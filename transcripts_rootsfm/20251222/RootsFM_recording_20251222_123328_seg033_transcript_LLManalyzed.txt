Reports are that passers-by came up on the body sometime around 7 o'clock and immediately alerted the authorities. Officers responded to the scene and found the man lying face down in the bushes off the roadway. The body was partially decomposed and fully clothed. Police say the man was wearing a pair of water boots and had a knapsack on his back at the time he was discovered. The area was secured and processed by investigators before the body was removed. No immediate identification has been made and the police have not yet indicated whether foul play is suspected. A post-mortem examination is to be carried out to determine the cause of death.
