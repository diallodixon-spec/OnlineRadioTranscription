20251222 124233 All St. Catherine Court matters are now being heard in Kingston, following damage caused by Hurricane Melissa, according to the Court Administration Division. Speaking with JIS Director of Client Services, Communication and Information at the Division, Khadysh Jarrett Fletcher, said the Rodney Memorial Building, which houses the court's administrative operations, suffered significant water damage during the hurricane. She explained that flooding compromised sections of the flooring, creating a safety risk for staff and members of the public. Based on advice from technical experts, the building has been deemed unsafe for occupation, prompting the relocation of circuit court settings. Jared Fletcher said adjustments have been made to accommodate administrative staff and to allow parish court matters to continue.
