According to reports, police were responding to information about illicit activity in the area when they conducted a search at a premises at about 5.30 a.m. Eleven women, nine men, and three boys, all Haitians, were found at the location. They were processed by the Kingston Eastern Divisional Intelligence Unit and later handed over to the Passport Immigration and Citizenship Agency for further investigations.
