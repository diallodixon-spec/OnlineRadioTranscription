A man who was a person of interest in multiple murders and shootings across several police divisions in Kingston and St. Andrew was this morning fatally shot during an intelligence-led operation by the police in Augustown. The deceased has been identified as Deandre Williams, also called Yankee, and street life of Nanny Boulevard in Nannyville, St. Andrew. The police say he was killed during a reported confrontation with a team at 1A Augustown Road, bottom of the Kingston 7 area. One 9mm Taurus pistol with a magazine containing 10 cartridges was reportedly seized from Williams. The police say Williams, a top-tier member of the nighttime brawling group and the African Gardens Vietnam gang, was being sought in connection with murders and shootings committed across the St. Andrew Central, St. Andrew North, St. Andrew South and Kingston Eastern Divisions.
