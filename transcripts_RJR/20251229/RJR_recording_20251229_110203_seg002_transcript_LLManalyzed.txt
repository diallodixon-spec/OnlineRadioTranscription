At least three locations are affected where the final layers of pavement are yet to be completed. The areas of concern include the Yallis Main Road near the Hatties Road intersection, the Wharf Road and Licensed Road intersection in Moran Bay, and Licensed Main Road near Licensed Primary school. Motorists say the unfinished surfaces pose serious safety risks. Their concerns were heightened last week when a motorcyclist was involved in a crash on Wharf Road due to the uneven roadway. Motorists are now appealing to the National Works Agency and China Harbour Engineering Company to complete the rehabilitation work before applying road markings urging the authorities to prioritise safety over appearance.
