AfREXIM Bank says it will engage with relevant agencies and authorities to explore the possibility of developing country programs to support recovery projects in the two countries. The bank's Caribbean office has been mandated to engage with both governments to explore ways of providing further support towards the reconstruction process. It says it will also continue to work with Caribbean governments to explore long-term financing solutions that strengthen resilience, restore livelihoods, and reinforce critical infrastructure for the future.
