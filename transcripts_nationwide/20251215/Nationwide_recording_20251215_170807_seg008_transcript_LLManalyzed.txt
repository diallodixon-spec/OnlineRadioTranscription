St. James, which was once the most murderous parish in the country, is two weeks away from recording its lowest murder tally in more than two decades. Commanding officer for the parish's police division, Senior Superintendent Aaron Samuels, revealed that the parish's murder tally stood at 52, as at December 11. Nationwide news spoke to West Central St James MP Marlene Malahoo-Fort, who says she's pleased at the massive reduction. Robianne Williams has the details.
