The country's solid waste management capacity has been further strengthened following the handover of 10 new tipper trucks to the National Solid Waste Management Authority, NSWMA. The units were handed over by Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness during a ceremony earlier today. In his address at the event, Dr. Holness outlined that the new units arrive as the country continues to tackle the scale of destruction left behind by Hurricane Melissa, this is an impossible task. It is an overwhelming task, undoubtedly with the resources that we have, but it is not an impossible task.
