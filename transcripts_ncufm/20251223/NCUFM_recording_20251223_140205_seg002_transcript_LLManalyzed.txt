Swinging over to cricket, England are investigating reports of excessive drinking by players during a mid-series break in Noosa as Australia wrapped up an emphatic Ashes series win. The squad spent four nights in the Queensland beach town between the second and third tests, with media reports alleging some players drank heavily during the break and in the days leading up to it. A video circulating on social media appeared to show opener Ben Duckett intoxicated, though it has not been independently verified. England director of cricket Rob Key says the matter would be examined to separate fact from exaggeration, stressing that any suggestion of prolonged excessive drinking would be unacceptable.
