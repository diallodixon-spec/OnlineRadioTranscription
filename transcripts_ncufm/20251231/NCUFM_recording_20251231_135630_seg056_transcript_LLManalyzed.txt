Among them is the trucking of water to communities all over Western Jamaica. Water has been trucked to communities in Hanover including Horton Grove, Shetland Wood Housing Scheme, Copps and Nockalva. In Westmoreland, areas served included Bethel Town, Darlingston Retreat, Red Road and Petersville. In St. Elizabeth, a trucking covered Providence, Longwood, Beatles Heights and Boulevard, Brighton, Retirement Road, Bellevue Housing Scheme, phases 1 to 3, Potts Dam to Mulvern and Mountainside Square along with Cambridge in St. James. The NWC has also provided an amnesty for micro, small and medium-sized enterprises, MSMEs, intended to give these entities an opportunity to clear their outstanding water bills and restore their accounts with the Commission.
