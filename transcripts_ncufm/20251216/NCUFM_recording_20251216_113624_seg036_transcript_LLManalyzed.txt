20251216 113624 When the oil is hot, add your bay leaf or mint leaf and rosemary leaf. Allow to seep for 10 minutes over low heat. Turn the heat off and leave the pan on the stove uncovered to seep for another 30 minutes to an hour. At this point the treatment is at a liquid state but we want it to be solid. So after 30 minutes or an hour strain out the leaves and discard. Pour your liquid into your jar and cover the jar tightly and place it in the refrigerator overnight to harden.
