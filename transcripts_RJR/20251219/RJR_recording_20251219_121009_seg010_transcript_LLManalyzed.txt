The Manchester Municipal Corporation says there will be no penalties for persons who are late with their property tax payments for November and December. Chairman of the Corporation and Mayor of Mandeville, Donovan Mitchell, says the reprieve is to assist residents who were affected by the passage of Hurricane Melissa in late October. Mr. Mitchell told the monthly meeting of the Municipal Corporation recently that collection officers have been instructed not to serve summ his summons or to take anybody to court, but to ask the persons who can afford to pay their property tax because that is what is keeping things flowing.
