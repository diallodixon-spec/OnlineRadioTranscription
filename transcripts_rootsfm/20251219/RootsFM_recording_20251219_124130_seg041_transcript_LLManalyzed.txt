More than 200 households in Riverton Meadows, formerly known as Riverton City, are now connected to the National Grid through a major electricity access project aimed at cutting illegal connections and improving safety in St. Andrew Western. The initiative, led by the Jamaica Public Service in partnership with the Jamaica Social Investment Fund, was officially closed out on Thursday during a community event at the Riverton Meadows Baptist Church. JPS says electricity theft has been widespread in the area, contributing to unsafe wiring, frequent outages, and heavy financial losses.
