Stay tuned to today's feature to hear the range of measures being employed by both agencies to ease hardship and support recovery in some of the hardest hit areas. Even as it continues working to repair its damaged water systems in western Jamaica in the aftermath of Hurricane Melissa, the National Water Commission, NWC, continues to implement short-term measures to ensure persons in need can access the precious commodity. Among them is the trucking of water to communities all over Western Jamaica. Water has been trucked to communities in Hanover, including Horton Grove, Shetland Wood Housing Scheme, Copps and Nakalva.
