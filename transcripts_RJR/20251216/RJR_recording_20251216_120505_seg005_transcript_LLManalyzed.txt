The Student's Loan Bureau is reporting encouraging participation in its recently launched Debt Reset Program, with approximately 3,600 clients having already applied. Of this group, 800 have sought the arrears credit, while 2,900 have applied for the good-standing credit of $100,000. Overall, some 25,000 lonies stand to benefit from the initiative. The SLB says more than 6,000 clients have been onboarded through the customer portal which gives them access to confirm that the benefit has been or will be applied to their loan accounts. Under the Debt Recent Program, the SLB is offering a limited-time initiative to assist borrowers with targeted loan accounts that are in arrears as at October 31, 2025.
