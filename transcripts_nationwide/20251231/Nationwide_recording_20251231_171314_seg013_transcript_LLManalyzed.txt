20251231 171314 In regional news now, in Trinidad and Tobago, Minister of Homeland Security Roger Alexander says he will be bringing a proposal to Cabinet in the new year to recommend silent-only fireworks. Both Houses of Parliament unanimously passed the fireworks legislation, the Summary Offenses Amendment Act 2025, on December 16. Under the law, fireworks may only be discharged with a permit issued by the police commissioner, except on public holidays between 8 pm and 9 pm, and on December 31, between 11.30 pm and 12.30 am. But Minister Alexander says citizens are not obeying the law.
