Home Affairs Minister Onij Walrond says the move comes after authorities found that a significant number of vehicles on the country's roads were operating outside existing tint regulations. Under the new motor vehicles and road traffic tint regulations, which took effect on January 1 next year, motorists will be allowed to use some level of tint without the need for special exemptions. However, clear limits have now been set. The regulations prohibit tint on the front windscreen, except for a visor not exceeding six inches from the top. Front side windows must allow at least 25% visible light transmission, while rear side windows and the rear windscreen must allow a minimum of 20%. Mirrored or reflective tint will not be permitted.
