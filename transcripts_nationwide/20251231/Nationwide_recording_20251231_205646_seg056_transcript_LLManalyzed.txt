20251231 205646 Squash pleads guilty in U.S. firearm case. Dancehall artist Andres Squash Whitico, also known as Six Boss, has taken a plea deal in the United States. He pleaded guilty to being an illegal alien in possession of a firearm while the more serious charge of possessing an automatic weapon was dismissed. The deal means prosecutors will recommend a lighter sentence, though the court has the final say. Squash faces up to 15 years in prison, three years of supervised release, and a US $250,000 fine along with possible deportation. Court documents state in February he was seen accepting a firearm in Miami Beach and police later recovered a loaded Glock 19 from his vehicle. He reportedly entered the US illegally in 2021.
