If you plan to consume alcohol during the holiday celebrations, ensure your firearm is properly stored beforehand. Alcohol impairs your judgment, reaction time, and decision-making ability, which can lead to accidents or unintended harm if you are carrying a firearm. To avoid dangerous situations, always secure your weapon in a safe place before drinking. Remember, a part of responsible firearm ownership means knowing when to lock it away.
