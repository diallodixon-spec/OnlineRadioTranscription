20251218 170405 In other news, Minister of State in the Ministry of National Security and Peace, Juliet Cuthberth Flynn, has praised members of the Jamaican Stabulary Force for going beyond law enforcement to actively build peace within communities. She was speaking yesterday at the Lasco JCF Saluting or Heroes 2025 award ceremony, which recognized police officers for outstanding community service and leadership. Minister Cuthberth Flynn said the government intends to strengthen partnerships with police-led community projects as part of its wider peace and security strategy, noting that officers are increasingly serving as mentors, advocates and community partners. All six finalists had one common vision and that is to foster a more peaceful community and of course by extension a peaceful society.
