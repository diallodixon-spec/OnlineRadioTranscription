20251219 170304 Jamaica Conference, CJC is thanking donors both in Jamaica and in the diaspora who are helping in the recovery process after Hurricane Melissa. President of the Central Jamaica Conference, Pastor Neville Barrett expressed his thanks while he encouraged the congregants to pray for our members and associates on the island of Jamaica and here at Central Jamaica Conference and our members in the diaspora, many of you are watching online, for responding so briskly to calls for relief and restoration. You send resources, you send up your prayers, you call us and we appreciate the hurricane affected CJC and how resilient its members are.
