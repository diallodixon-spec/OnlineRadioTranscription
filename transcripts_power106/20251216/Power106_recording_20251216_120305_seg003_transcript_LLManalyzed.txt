Residents in five parishes who sustained water and building damage to their homes during Hurricane Melissa in October have started to receive assistance under the National Energy Recertification Program. The program was implemented by the government with funds directed from the National Energy Poverty Reduction Project to regularize and restore safe electrical access to homes affected by the hurricane. It will run to March 31 next year and executed through the Minister of Energy.
