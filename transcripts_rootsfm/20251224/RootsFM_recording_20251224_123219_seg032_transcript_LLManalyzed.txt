Andre Daniels of Middle Quarters has been charged with two counts of murder, wounding with intent, illegal possession of a firearm, illegal possession of ammunition, and assault at common law. The charges relate to an incident on June 9, 2011, along Jarrett Lane in Middle Quarters. Two young men, 24-year-old Andrew Gray and 19-year-old Dave Davis, were fatally shot during the attack. A woman who was inside the dwelling at the time was also injured. Police say Daniels managed to evade capture for more than a decade, but was apprehended during a targeted operation carried out earlier this month. He has since been formally charged. Head of the St. Elizabeth Police Division has commended investigators for their persistence in pursuing the case and bringing it to this stage.
