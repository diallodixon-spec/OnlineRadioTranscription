Now for persons who may not know about the Global Jamaica Diaspora Council, it is a representative body created under the Jamaica's national diaspora policy to give Jamaicans living overseas a formal voice in national development. The council advises the government of Jamaica on issues affecting the diaspora, including economic investment, social development, and engagement with Jamaicans abroad. Recently, the global Jamaica Diaspora Council elections were postponed and rescheduled to allow more time for registration, nomination, and participation across diaspora regions.
