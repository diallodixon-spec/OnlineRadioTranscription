20260102 100200 In health news, State Minister in the Ministry of Health and Wellness, Crystal Lee, is encouraging fathers to continue to play an active role in child-rearing and to support the mothers of their children. Speaking at the Maternal Health Outreach event at Mount Carey Health Care in St. Elizabeth recently, Lee noted that sharing responsibilities in child-rearing can lead to healthier families, ensuring the well-being of both mothers and children. The outreach marked the sixth in a series of interventions aimed at assisting expectant and new mothers in parishes most affected by Hurricane Melissa.
