20251219 210103 The annual Potters' Fair has sat at its very own nexus of artistry and commerce for decades. A unique, truly one-of-a-kind opportunity for local artists and artisans to connect with their market, to share inspiration, access direct feedback, and really just revel in the atmosphere found at the Jamaica Horticultural Society grounds each and every Christmas. Tis the season to enjoy a truly artistic experience. Come with us to the Potters' Fair. A small corner of the vast 200-acre Hope Botanical Gardens, the Jamaica Horticultural Society Showgrounds has been the home of the Potters' fair since its move from the forestry department. What has survived the move is the consistency of the product, the caliber of experience, the energy on the fairgrounds.
