But generally speaking, properties are not sold furnished unless expressly agreed. And more often than not, where the chattels are being sold with the property, the seller will charge a separate amount for that. Can a landlord ask someone to act on his or her behalf with written permission given to apply for utilities? Yes, that is possible. You just have to be careful though with how it's worded because some utility companies are very specific. I've had instances where a person sends a letter and the utility companies don't accept it. So ensure it is notarized. If all else fails, have an attorney do it for you.
