Because the heart is the control center for lives, it's our thoughts, our choices, our attitudes, actions, everything flows from what we allow to settle there. And if the heart is divided, your integrity becomes fragile. If the heart is surrendered, integrity becomes natural. You see, living with integrity, it means allowing God to shape who we are before we focus on what we do. It means aligning our inner life with our outward actions. When our private convictions match our public conduct, we walk in integrity. The challenge is though that the heart is constantly under pressure.
