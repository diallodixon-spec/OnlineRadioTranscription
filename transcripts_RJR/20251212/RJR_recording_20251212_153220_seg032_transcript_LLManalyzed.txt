20251212 153220 The National Housing Trust now has a hurricane relief loan for homeowners. NHD contributors who suffered damage to their property following the passage of Hurricane Melissa may be eligible for a special home improvement loan. Application forms are available at NHT's offices or via the website at www.nht.gov.jm. For more information, visit their website, contact your nearest NHT office, or dial toll-free 888-CALL-NHT. Conditions apply.
