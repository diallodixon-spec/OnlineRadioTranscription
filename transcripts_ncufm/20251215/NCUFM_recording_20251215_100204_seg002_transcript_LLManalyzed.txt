20251215 100204 In health news, Health Minister Dr. Christopher Tufton says the public should expect delays for elective surgeries after only three of 13 operating theaters assessed at five hospitals in western Jamaica were found functional. Hurricane Melissa caused major damage to the Black River, Falmouth, Savannah Lamar, Cornwall Regional and Noel Holmes Hospitals after it made a landfall on October 28. Speaking at a ministerial press briefing on Thursday, the Health Minister explained that the report is based on an assessment done at the operating theatre's capacity and equipment status across the five hospitals. A total of 13 operating theatres were reviewed across these facilities.
