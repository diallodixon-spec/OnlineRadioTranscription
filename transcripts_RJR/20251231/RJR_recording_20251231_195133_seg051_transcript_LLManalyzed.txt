The NWC has also provided an amnesty for micro, small and medium-sized enterprises, MSMEs, intended to give these entities an opportunity to clear their outstanding water bills and restore their accounts with the Commission. Under the program, a 50% write-off is being offered to small business customers within active accounts that have been disconnected for more than three months, and active accounts with debts of more than $30,000 for over 180 days. It was originally scheduled for September 1 to November 30, 2025, but was later extended to the end of the year. We know everybody is feeling some kind of effect from Hurricane Melissa and that is our way of giving back to the small businesses. Content creation and development manager at the NWC, Andrew Cannon.
