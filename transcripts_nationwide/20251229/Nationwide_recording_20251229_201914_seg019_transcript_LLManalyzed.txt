20251229 201914 Commissioner Beckford is encouraging members of the public and business operators that have fire hydrants on or near to their premises to contact the JFB to ensure they are being properly maintained. Commissioner of the Jamaica Fire Brigade, Stuart Beckford. To report any hydrant maintenance concerns, call the Hydrant Maintenance Section of Jamaica Fire Brigade at 876-878-4642 or send an email to HMS at jfb.gov.jm. You can also connect with the Fire Brigade on their social media pages on Facebook at Jamaica Fire Brigade and Instagram at thehandle at jamaicafirebrigade.
