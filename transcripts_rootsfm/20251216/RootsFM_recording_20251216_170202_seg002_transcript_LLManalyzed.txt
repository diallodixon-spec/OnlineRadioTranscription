Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness has reassured Jamaicans that there is no ban on parties or festive activities during the Christmas season, dismissing claims circulating on social media. Speaking with residents at the Petersfield High School shelter in Westmoreland during the Prime Minister's Christmas treat, Dr. Holness warned that misinformation can create unnecessary fear and undermine public order. He said the government supports entertainment activities once they are properly organized and meet public safety requirements.
