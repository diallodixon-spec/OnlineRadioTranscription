The Ministry of Health and Wellness has launched a maternal outreach program targeting pregnant and new mothers in areas affected by Hurricane Melissa. State Minister Crystal Lee says expectant and new mothers are among the most vulnerable after disasters and must receive continuous care and support. The program involves health workers visiting communities to ensure mothers and babies receive regular checkups, nutrition guidance, mental health support, and essential supplies.
