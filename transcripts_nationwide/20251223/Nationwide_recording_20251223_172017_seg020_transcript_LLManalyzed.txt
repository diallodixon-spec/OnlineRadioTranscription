20251223 172017 The Trump administration has announced that it will begin garnishing the wages of student loan borrowers who are in default early next year. On Tuesday, the department says it will send notices to approximately 1,000 borrowers during the week of January 7. It says more notices are to come at an increasing scale each month. Millions of borrowers are considered in default, meaning they are 270 days past due on their payments. The department must give borrowers 30 days notice before their wages can be garnished. The department says it will begin collection activities
