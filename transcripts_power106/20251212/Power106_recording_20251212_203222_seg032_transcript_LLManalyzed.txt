To take advantage of the Debt Reset program, borrowers must first register online with the SLB. This is done through the Bureau's website at www.slbja.com. Once they navigate to the page, they'll see the onboarding icon. They'll click on that and what it does is they'll be required to enter some key demographic information. Once that is done, the system will seek to authenticate that they are who they say they are. Once that it's done, they'll be able to access the website and start the application for the debt reset. The application process is a four-step system. Borrowers can choose to clear their arrears in one lump sum or they can schedule payments over the December to April period, giving them flexibility based on their financial circumstances.
