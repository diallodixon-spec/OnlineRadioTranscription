20251222 171208 Some people have died in separate motor vehicle crashes in Manchester, while a body has also been retrieved following a vehicle plunge into a river in St. Elizabeth. In Manchester, police say a motorcyclist and his female passenger were travelling along the Grove Town to Warwick Main Road in the early morning hours on Sunday when the motorcycle crashed. The motorcyclist, identified only by his alias Nico, was pronounced dead at hospital. The pillion rider was admitted for treatment. Hours later, another crash occurred along the New Green Road near Martins Hill. Police report that a Nissan motorcar carrying four occupants veered off the roadway and plunged into a gully. 58-year-old Judith Morgan later died at hospital. Her 30-year-old daughter and two grandchildren, aged three years and two months, were injured and hospitalized. Investigators say the driver lost control on a wet section of the road.
