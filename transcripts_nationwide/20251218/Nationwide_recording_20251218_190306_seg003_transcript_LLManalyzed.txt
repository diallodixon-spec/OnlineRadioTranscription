The initiative was formally announced by President Dr. Asburn Pinnock during the institution's annual Christmas Carol service. Dr. Pinnock emphasized that the Christmas season calls not only for celebration, but for intentional acts of goodwill that respond to real human need. Gifts collected through the toy drive will be delivered to communities in Western Jamaica, where many families and children remain vulnerable following the storm. The outreach will focus particularly on children, recognizing the emotional and psychological impact that natural disasters can have during the holiday season. The MICO is encouraging donors to contribute new or gently used toys, age-appropriate gifts, educational items, and other child-friendly supplies.
