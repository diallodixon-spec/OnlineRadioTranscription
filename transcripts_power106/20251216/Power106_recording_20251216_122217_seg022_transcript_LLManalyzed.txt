Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness says the government is examining how state-owned lands can be better organized and utilized to support structured communities while preserving critical agricultural areas. Addressing residents during a land titling ceremony at the La Covea Community Center in St. Elizabeth recently, Prime Minister Holness said there is a deliberate effort to bring government lands under closer review. He said that while several agencies already hold lands, careful planning is required to see how we can start, as we say in Jamaica, to cut up the land, to build structured communities, to make them available to the average Jamaican.
