20251223 160104 The Bank of Jamaica is reporting that it has temporarily waived the penalty charged to deposit-taking institutions for using the automated clearinghouse ACH platform to facilitate transactions over $1 million, arising from ongoing issues with the real-time gross transfers RTGS system. In a media release today, the Bank of Jamaica said some deposit-taking institutions are still experiencing challenges accrediting RTGS payments following the recent migration to the ISO 222 MX standard on December 15. To ease the impact, the BOJ will temporarily waive the $5,000 penalty on ACH transactions of $1 million and over. The waiver will cover Monday, December 15 to Wednesday, December 31.
