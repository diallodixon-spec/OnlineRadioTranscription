20251229 170606 In other news, the Jamaica Constabulary Force, JCF, is urging the public to refrain from carrying out illegal gun salutes during the New Year celebrations. Police say the practice is dangerous and unlawful, posing serious risks to life and property, and can result in severe injuries or death. The JCF warns that anyone involved in firing guns in celebration or facilitating the act will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law. As the holiday period continues, the police say they will maintain high visibility across communities and business centers island-wide to ensure public safety. Citizens are being encouraged to play their part by reporting any incidents of gun salutes to the police. Reports can be made by calling the 119 emergency number or contacting the nearest police station. The JCF says these measures are aimed at ensuring a safe and peaceful start to the new year for all Jamaicans.
