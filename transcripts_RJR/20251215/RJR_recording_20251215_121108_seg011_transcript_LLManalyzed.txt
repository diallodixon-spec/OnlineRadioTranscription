Minister of Culture, Gender, Entertainment, and Sport, Olivia Grange, has expressed sorrow at the sudden passing of Chief Executive Officer of the RGR Gleaner Communications Group, Anthony Smith. Mr. Smith died yesterday following a brief illness. Ms. Grange says Mr. Smith's death has left her deeply saddened as the media fraternity has lost another outstanding leader. She extended condolences to Mr. Smith's family, friends, and associates, as well as to the RGR Gleaner Communications Group. Opposition spokesperson on information, Nikesha Birchell, in extending condolence, described Mr. Smith as a steady and thoughtful leader who brought clarity, professionalism, and a strong sense of purpose to Jamaica's media landscape.
