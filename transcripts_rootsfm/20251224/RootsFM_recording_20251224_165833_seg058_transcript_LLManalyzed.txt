Police have identified the man whose body was found along a hall road in the community of Brokenhurst, Manchester on Monday morning. He is 41-year-old Everle Richards, a maintenance worker of Villa Road in Mandeville. Reports are that the body, which bore what appeared to be gunshot wounds, was discovered sometime after six o'clock by a passerby who alerted the authorities. Police were called to the scene shortly after. Head of the Manchester Police Division, Superintendent of Police Kerry Duncan, who was on location, said residents reported hearing loud explosions around 9 o'clock on Sunday night. However, police were not alerted at the time. Officers later cordoned off the area as forensic teams carried out the processing of the scene.
