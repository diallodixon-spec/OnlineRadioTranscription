**Interview with Kimona Myrie, Founder and CEO of Travel with Kimi**

In an inspiring conversation on the Hello Jamaica show, Queen Jojo sat down with Kimona Myrie, the founder and CEO of Travel with Kimi, a Jamaican-based travel and visa services company. Kimona shared her passion for helping others and her desire to make travel accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

Kimona emphasized that traveling is not just for the wealthy, and that with proper planning and guidance, anyone can explore the world. She highlighted the importance of preparation, particularly when it comes to visa applications, and noted that many people make mistakes by not providing adequate documentation. Travel with Kimi offers consultation services to help individuals prepare for visa applications, increasing their chances of approval.

When asked about affordable travel planning, Kimona advised that people should research and budget accordingly, and that her company can guide them in finding cost-effective options. She also emphasized the benefits of group travel, including meeting like-minded people, lower prices, and creating long-term friendships.

For first-time travelers, Kimona offered three must-know tips: booking direct flights, having both cash and cards, and advising banks before traveling to avoid account holds. She also recommended purchasing an eSIM card for staying connected while abroad.

Throughout the conversation, Kimona's enthusiasm and expertise shone through, inspiring listeners to take the leap and explore the world. As she said, "I go so you know," emphasizing her commitment to helping others experience the joy of travel. With Travel with Kimi, individuals can turn their travel dreams into stress-free reality, and Kimona's passion and dedication are making that possible.
