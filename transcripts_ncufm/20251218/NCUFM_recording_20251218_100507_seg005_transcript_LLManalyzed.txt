20251218 100507 According to portfolio minister Dr. Christopher Tufton, the wellness agenda of the ministry recognizes the importance of working in key settings which are likely to reach most of the population and involve important stakeholders within the community. He says the ministry remains committed to using research evidence to guide its policies in improving the wellness of persons within society. Minister Tufton adds that the wellness areas of focus for the ministry include wellness in workplace, schools, homes, communities, and additional persons living with non-communicable diseases, NCDs, and risk factors, as well as mental health and maternal health.
