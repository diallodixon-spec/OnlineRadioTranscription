20251230 170304 A 41-year-old security guard has been charged following the seizure of a firearm during a police operation in St. Andrew last week. The accused has been identified as Kevin Smith of Seaview Gardens. Police say he was charged with possession of a prohibited weapon, possession of ammunition, and unauthorized use of blue lights. Reports indicate that just after 11.30 on the night of December 26th, police observed Smith driving a red Kia Seltos along the roadway in Zadie Gardens. The vehicle was reportedly fitted with flashing blue lights. Officers signaled him to stop and he complied. Police say that after Smith exited the vehicle, officers noticed a bulge on both sides of his waist. A search reportedly revealed a Taurus G2C firearm with an attached magazine containing four rounds of ammunition. A separate Glock magazine with one.40 caliber cartridge was also seized.
