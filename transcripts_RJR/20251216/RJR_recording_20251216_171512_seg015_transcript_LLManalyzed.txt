20251216 171512 A man accused of killing his boss and dumping his body in St Catherine last last month was remanded today when he appeared before the St. Catherine Parish Court. Lawyers for 35-year-old Rodin Allison applied for bail today, but this was not entertained by the judge, who indicated that the issue is for the Corporate Area Gun Court. Allison was then remanded to appear in Gun Court on January 20, 2026. He is facing charges of murder, possession of prohibited weapon, unauthorized possession of ammunition, and using a prohibited weapon to commit a scheduled offense following the death of his boss, Khalil Jerome Martin.
