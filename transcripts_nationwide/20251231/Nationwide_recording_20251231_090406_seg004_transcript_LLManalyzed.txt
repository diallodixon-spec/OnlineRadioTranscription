In the meantime, the National Irrigation Commission, NIC, has also stepped up to support its customers in these challenging times. The commission has provided over 3,000 of its customers with free, non-portable water to support agricultural recovery activities in areas affected by Hurricane Melissa. Public education specialist with the NIC Christine Forbes says the move forms part of the commission's corporate responsibility to serve communities and touch lives. Schools, police stations and residents also benefited under the initiative.
