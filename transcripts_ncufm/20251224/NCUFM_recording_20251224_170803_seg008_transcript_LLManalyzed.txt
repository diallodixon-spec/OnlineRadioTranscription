Assistant Commissioner of Police Dr. Gary McKenzie and Senior Superintendent Delia Garrick made the call during a Jamaica Information Service think tank. Police are also warning against openly discussing financial plans in public spaces or on mobile phones, noting that such conversations can attract criminals.
