President and CEO of the Jamaica Public Service Company, Hugh Grant, says JPS crews and their overseas partners have ramped up the restoration of electricity to communities still in the dark more than a month after Hurricane Melissa slammed into the island. Speaking today at the official commissioning of the Emergency Mobile Power Generation Unit in Treasure Beach, St. Elizabeth, Mr. Grant said the company is making progress in restoring power supply to the affected communities in western Jamaica. GPS is working every hour of every day until power is restored to all communities in this great nation of Jamaica.
