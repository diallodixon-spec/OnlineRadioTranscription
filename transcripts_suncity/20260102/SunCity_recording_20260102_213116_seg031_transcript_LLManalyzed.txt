Friends of Region 6 was established to support educational institutions within the Ministry of Education, Skills, Youth and Information's Region 6, which covers St. Catherine and parts of Clarendon. It does so through student and teachers awards, relief efforts, food and supply assistance and counseling services, among other initiatives. Among the awardees this year were Teacher of the Year winner Camille Brown from Enid Bennett High School, Support Staff Member of the Year winner Damian Cardis from Old Arbor High School, Region 6 Employee of the Year, Community Relations Education Officer at the Ministry of Education, Kareen Reed-Clark and Principal of the Year, Greater Portmore Highs, Ricardo Ross.
