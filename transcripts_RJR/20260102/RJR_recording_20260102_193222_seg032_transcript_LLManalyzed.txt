The National Housing Trust has begun accepting applications for its 2026 refund period, allowing qualified contributors to claim refunds for contributions made in 2018 and earlier. The NHT says applications opened on January 1 and can be submitted at any time during the year. All mortgages whose accounts are up to date are eligible to apply for a refund of their 2018 contributions. Refund applications must be submitted exclusively online through the official NHT website or the NHD online platform. Contributors are being reminded to ensure all information entered is accurate, particularly names, banking details, and contribution records, as errors can delay processing or require applications to be resubmitted.
