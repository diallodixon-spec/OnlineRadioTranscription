Dennis Zulu, United Nations resident coordinator in Jamaica. Meanwhile, Mr. Zulu says the UN is recommitting its support to Jamaica as the country transitions to the recovery stage. The scale and cost of recovery are immense. Rebuilding homes, restoring infrastructure and vitalizing livelihoods and strengthening resilience will require substantial financial resources over an extended period. For this reason, Jamaica recovery cannot rest on national efforts alone. It will require the collective effort of bilateral and multilateral partners, international financial institutions, the private sector and indeed the wider development community
