20251225 090203 As this year drew to a close, Jamaica was preparing to celebrate what had otherwise been a strong and encouraging year. We had faced challenges, but we were moving forward with confidence, purpose, and hope. Then in late October, everything changed. Category 5 Hurricane Melissa, the strongest and most devastating hurricane ever to strike our island, descended upon us near the very end of the season and turned our world upside down. In a matter of hours, lulls were disrupted and entire communities, particularly across the western parishes, were left shattered. Homes were destroyed, agriculture was crippled, schools, churches, hospitals and businesses were damaged or lost, and the emotional toll has been profound.
