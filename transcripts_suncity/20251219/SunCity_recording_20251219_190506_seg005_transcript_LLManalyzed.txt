Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness is encouraging greater private sector participation in the National Hurricane Melissa recovery effort as the scale of need remains significant. The Prime Minister made the appeal during the handover of building supplies to the Office of Disaster Preparedness and Emergency Management, ODPEM, by Chemtech Development and Construction Limited yesterday. Valid at over $50 million, the supplies will go towards the rebuilding of roofs and homes across parishes most severely impacted by the hurricane.
