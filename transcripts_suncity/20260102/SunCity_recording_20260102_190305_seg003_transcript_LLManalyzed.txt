The National Housing Trust has begun accepting applications for its 2026 refund period, allowing qualified contributors to claim refunds for contributions made in 2018 and earlier. The NHT says applications opened on January 1 and can be submitted at any time during the year. All mortgages whose accounts are up to date are eligible to apply for a refund of their 2018 contributions.
