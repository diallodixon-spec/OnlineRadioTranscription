20251218 122418 The Jamaica Constabulary Force, JCF, in collaboration with the Lasco Chin Foundation, has recognized 158 police officers for their contributions to community policing through mentorship and youth engagement. The officers were recognized at the 2025-2026 Saluting Our Heroes Awards Ceremony at the Jamaica Pegasus Hotel on December 17. State Minister in the Ministry of National Security and Peace, Juliet Cuthbert Flynn, who spoke at the event, lauded the awardees for safety has transformed lives, strengthened the fabric of our society, and your resilience and unwavering resolve inspires us all.
