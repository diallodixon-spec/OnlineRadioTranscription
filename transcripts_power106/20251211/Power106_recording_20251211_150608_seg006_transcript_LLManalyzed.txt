Dr. Reddy's Labor Retreats, a pharmaceutical company, has donated medication valid at $215,000 to support Jamaica's response to Hurricane Melissa. The National Health Fund, the NHF, is the recipient of the donation, handed over November 25 at the NHF head office in New Kingston. The medications include antibiotics, supplements and medications to treat chronic illnesses and total 42,500 packages that will be distributed
