Reggae recording artist Hezran has partnered with the Rotary Club of Ligonier Plains for the Save the Children campaign school tour. Named after his 2022 single Save the Children, the initiative uses music to inspire students while engaging parents through workshops held during Parents Week. The tour began at Louise Bennet-Coverly Primary and continued at several schools, including New Providence Primary and Haley-Selassie High. Rotary President Dr. Carolyn Graham says plans are underway to make the program an annual event combining music, literacy and anti-bullying initiatives to promote a stronger society.
