Many of us got socks, cheap mugs or those generic candles and hopefully you didn't get a scale, unwanted pet or gym membership. but a few of us scored big with unique works of art, jewelry, exotic plants, or otherwise fascinating curios found at the annual Potters' Fair. If you're in that select group, your gift has a story behind it, one of resilience and audacious hope.
