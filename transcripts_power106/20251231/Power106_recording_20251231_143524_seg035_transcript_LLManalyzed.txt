20251231 143524 The Honorable John Golding O.J., Professor of Tropical Orthopaedics and Rehabilitation in the Department of Surgery at the UWI has been made a Knight Bachelor for his services to medicine in the Queen's New Year's Honours List. Born in London, Professor Golding came to Jamaica in 1953 as a senior lecturer in orthopaedics at the UWI, the University of the West Indies. Prior to this appointment, he was senior registrar at the Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital in London.
