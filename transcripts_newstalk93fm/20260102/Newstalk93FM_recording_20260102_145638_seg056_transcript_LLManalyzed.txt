20260102 145638 Treasure Beach, St. Elizabeth have made quite the haul. Over $7 million in supplies and equipment. Part of Hurricane Melissa recovery activities, the supplies were provided by the National Commercial Bank Foundation and in partnership with the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining. Agriculture Minister Floyd Green says the initiative provided about 100 farmers with vouchers valued at $50,000 each for fertilizer, weed whackers, sprays and other tools. Another 100 million dollars of support today to our farmers and fishers, giving them everything from direct inputs like seeds and fertiliser to equipment to things like fishing wire, binding wire and also ropes so that our fishers can go back out as quickly as possible, so that our farmers can go back out quickly as possible.
