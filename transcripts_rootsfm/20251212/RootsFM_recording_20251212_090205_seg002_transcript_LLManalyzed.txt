2025-12-12 10:31:56 The Spectrum Management Authority presents Unity in AdiCity with their staging of a community wellness fair this Friday, December 12, and you guessed it, Roots FM will be there all day to bring you the happenings. It will be a day of help and healing, providing love for all in the Spectrum Management Authority's parking lot at 13 to 19 Harbour Street between 8 a.m. and 4 p.m. It will be a day of unification and upliftment celebrating resilience as a community amidst the challenges. There will be blood pressure screenings, eye tests and HIV testing provided by doctors and nurses from the University Hospital of the World.
