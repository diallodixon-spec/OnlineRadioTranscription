Charged are a 15-year-old boy of a Kingston 5 address and a 24-year-old Neville McDermott, a labourer of Kingston 16. The pair have been slapped with charges including possession of a prohibited weapon and the unauthorised possession of ammunition. Reports are that sometime after 11 in the night, a police team was on operation in the area when they signaled the rider of a motorcycle accompanied by a pillion to stop. The command was ignored and the police pursued the motorcycle. The rider of the motorcycle later stopped along the roadway. The police observed that a firearm had fallen to the ground and the pair were immediately arrested and the weapon seized by the police.
