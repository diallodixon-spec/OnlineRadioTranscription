Executive Director Audley Gordon said cleanup efforts are expected to be fast-tracked following the government's donation of 10 Tipper trucks valued at nearly $200 million. He noted that the additional trucks are timely, especially with increased waste generation during the Christmas period, but added that more equipment is still needed.
