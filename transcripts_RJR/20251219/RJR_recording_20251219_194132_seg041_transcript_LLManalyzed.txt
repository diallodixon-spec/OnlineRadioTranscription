Tis the season to enjoy a truly artistic experience. Come with us to the Potters' Fair. A small corner of the vast 200-acre Hope Botanical Gardens, the Jamaica Horticultural Society Showgrounds has been the home of the Potters' Fair since its move from the forestry department. What has survived the move is the consistency of the product, the caliber of experience, the energy on the fairgrounds. Amidst the throng of artists, artisans, collectors, enthusiastic buyers, and the merely curious, the buzz of the Potters' Fair, while distinctly felt, can be somewhat hard to describe. Patron and former mental health specialist Anthony Allen describes his experience as spiritual.
