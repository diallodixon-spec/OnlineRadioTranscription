20251231 190606 Now the news. The Ministry of Labour and Social Security has further intensified its National Household Damage Assessment operations, completing some 56,267 assessments so far. Portfolio Minister Pernal Charles Jr. says multi-agency teams consisting of approximately 540 trained assessors are being deployed daily using the Jamaica Household Damage Impact and Needs Assessment, JHDINA, platform. He notes, however, that this is being ramped up even further through a maximum deployment strategy that proposes to deploy 600 assessors across the six most severely affected parishes to complete the remaining 117,000.
