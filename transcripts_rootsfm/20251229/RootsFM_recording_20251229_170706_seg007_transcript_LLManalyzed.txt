Road safety is again under the spotlight following a series of crashes reported across the island over the Christmas weekend, leaving several people injured and others dead. In St. Thomas, two people were hospitalized early Sunday morning after their vehicle crashed into a utility pole along the Morant Main Road. Reports are that around 5 a.m., the car was traveling through an area known as Center Cut when it collided with the pole. Residents say the road is currently under construction and have raised concerns that the pole remains in the roadway without adequate lighting or warning signs. They are calling on the authorities to address what they describe as a serious hazard.
