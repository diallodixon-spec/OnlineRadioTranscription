Hurricane Melissa has made it clear that resilience is not only built with concrete and steel, but more so, it must be built with the law, with data, and with planning. Where land ownership is uncertain, planning breaks down. Where planning breaks down, vulnerabilities grow. And where vulnerabilities grow, disasters multiply. Our mission is to break that chain. Land titling therefore sits at the heart of national security. When communities lack formal tenure, criminal groups fill the vacuum.
