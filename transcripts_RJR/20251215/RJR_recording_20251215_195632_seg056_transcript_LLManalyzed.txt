20251215 195632 When you think of first aid, certain catchphrases come to mind, right? Like every second counts. And the truth is, with first aid, there is an emphasis on quick action, preparedness, and calm. The three P's come to mind. What are they? Well, preserve life, prevent further injury, promote recovery. And they emphasize the importance of acting fast to mitigate the long-term effects of trauma. And while this usually relates to physical trauma, it is just as applicable to trauma of a psychological nature. Towards this end, the Ministry of Health and Wellness is training 3,000 individuals in psychological first aid to assist the emotional support recovery of Jamaicans impacted by Hurricane Melissa. Even as the place is starting to get green again, the ministry hasn't let the grass grow under their feet.
