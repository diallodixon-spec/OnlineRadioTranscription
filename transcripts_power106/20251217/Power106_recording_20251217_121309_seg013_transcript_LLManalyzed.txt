20251217 121309 Champion Oblique Seville leads a list of nine men nominated for the RGR Gleno Sports Foundation National Sportsman of the Year Award for 2025. The full list of nominees was announced at the launch moments ago. Seville won his first individual global medal when he dismissed a quality field in Tokyo to take the men's 100m in a personal best 9.77 seconds. Silver medalist Kishane Thompson has also been nominated on a list where seven of the eight nominees are from track and field. The other nominees are Orlando Bennett, Tajay Gale, Bradley Vell, Tyler Mason, Wayne Pinnock and Raymond Richards, while grand senior world champion veteran Anthony T.J. Johnson is the only non-track and field nominee. Johnson created history by winning the country's first ever gold medal at the International Practice Shooting Confederation IPSC Handgun World Championships in South Africa when he took gold in September.
