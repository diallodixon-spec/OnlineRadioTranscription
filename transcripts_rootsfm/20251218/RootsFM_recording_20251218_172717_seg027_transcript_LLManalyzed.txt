20251218 172717 The Kingston Creative Art Walk Festival is back and this time we're celebrating the holidays with a grand market edition. Come downtown Kingston Church Street and Water Lane on Sunday December 21 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. when headline a Grammy-nominated singer Mortimer takes the main stage. Enjoy an entire day of activities for free, the best local artisans and gifts for Christmas shopping, eat delicious meals at the food court, and catch supplies with paint and sip. Discover downtown on the hop-on hop-up bus, visiting the National Gallery, Jamaica Music Museum, Trench Town, and the Mobile Skate Ring. The festival is brought to you by the Jamaica Tourist Board, the Tourism Enhancement Fund, the Kingston and St. Andrew Municipal Corporation, Catherine's Peak, Great Wall Motors, Stewart's Automotive Group, and Main Events.
