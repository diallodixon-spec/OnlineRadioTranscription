With only days away from Christmas and festivities in some quarters in full swing, the reality is that Christmas does not put a stop on people's suffering, whether at the hands of Hurricane Melissa or financial strain. There are, however, helpful strategies that persons can apply to better manage their mental state and find hope. Psychotherapist Adélie Fraser suggests that taking a moment to check in with yourself is an important first step. One of the first things persons may want to do is acknowledge what they're feeling, whether it is sadness, it is anxiety, it is anger, whatever it is, they want to acknowledge what those feelings are.
