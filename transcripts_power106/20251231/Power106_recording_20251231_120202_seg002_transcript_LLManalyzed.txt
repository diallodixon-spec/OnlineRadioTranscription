The 1% excise tax on remittances sent from the United States takes effect tomorrow, but managing director of New York-based Caribbean Immigration Services, Irwin Clear, is not anticipating a significant impact on inflows into Jamaica. Mr. Clare says he does not believe that the tax will cause Jamaicans in the diaspora to curtail the money they will send to their relatives.
