20251231 091714 I think a little bit of clarity is needed here. God does not want us to be poor in no way at all. In fact, we are rich in the Lord. However, there is a characteristic of the poor that we must strive to adapt as it relates to our spiritual life. And that characteristic is dependence. The poor are poor because they lack independence. They can't survive on their own and regularly need assistance to live. To be poor in spirit means recognizing or complete dependence on God, acknowledging our spiritual need and or inability to be saved without Him. No, this is not weakness, it is wisdom.
