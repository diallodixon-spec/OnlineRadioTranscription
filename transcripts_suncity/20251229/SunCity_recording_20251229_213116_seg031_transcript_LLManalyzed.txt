Without a constant water supply, firefighters would need to make multiple trips to replenish their water. To avoid this, the Jamaica Fire Brigade, JSB, maintains fire hydrants across the island through its Hydrant Maintenance Crew. The members of this crew are responsible for mapping hydrants, assessment and repair, servicing and repair of some of these items because the numbers that we are seeing in terms of those that are in need of servicing and repair far outstrip the resources that we have internally.
