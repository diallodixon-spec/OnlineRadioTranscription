Meanwhile, the Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining is procuring 100,000 broiler chickens for distribution to farmers as part of efforts to rebuild the poultry industry. Portfolio Minister Floyd Green says the poultry industry lost approximately 700,000 broiler birds and 458,000 hens following the passage of Hurricane Melissa. The procurement of the birds will also help to restart egg and chicken meat production on the island.
