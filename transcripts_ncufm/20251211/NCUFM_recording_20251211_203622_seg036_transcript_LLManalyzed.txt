Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness is calling on private developers to support Jamaica's rebuilding efforts by turning their construction expertise towards developing affordable housing. Dr. Holness says the government is ready to partner with contractors to build low-income housing solutions for displaced families and individuals as the country enters the reconstruction phase post Hurricane Melissa.
