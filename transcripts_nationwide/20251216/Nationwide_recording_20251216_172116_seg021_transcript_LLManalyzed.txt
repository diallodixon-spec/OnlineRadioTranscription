Court documents reveal that the transfer of shares in Gourstew, which were owned by Butch Stewart, was attempted by one of his sons, businessman Robert Bobby Stewart and attorney Dimitri Singh. Concerns were raised by Adam Stewart about the lawfulness of the transaction. The feud over Butch Stewart's estate has pitted Adam, who's the heir apparent, and his sister Jamie against their brother, Bobby Stewart, and several executors of the estate.
