Barbados has signed the protocol to become a member of the Geneva-based Advisory Center on the World Trade Organization, LOR, ACWL. The ACWL advises its developing country members and least developed countries on all issues relating to WTO law. It provides free legal advice and training on WTO law and provides support in WTO dispute settlement proceedings at discounted rates.
