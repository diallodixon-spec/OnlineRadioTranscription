20251225 171208 In regional news, Inter-Caribbean Airways has announced new non-stop air services linking Tortola with Barbados and St. Martin beginning March 8, 2026. The airline will operate seven weekly flights between Tortola's Terransby-Lexham International Airport and Grantley-Adams International Airport in Barbados. Four of those flights will be non-stop, while three weekly services will operate via St. Martin, providing direct connections between Tortola and Princess Juliana International Airport. The new routes are expected to significantly improve regional connectivity, giving travelers in Tortola easier access to Barbados and onward connections to destinations such as Guyana, Grenada, St. Lucia, Trinidad and St. Vincent. Passengers from Barbados and the southern Caribbean will also benefit from improved access to the northern Caribbean.
