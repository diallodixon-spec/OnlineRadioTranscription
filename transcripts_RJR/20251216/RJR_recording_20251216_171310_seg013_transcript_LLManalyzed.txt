20251216 171310 The Area One Police says it is scaling back administrative duties to put more boots on the ground as the Yuletide season heats up, even as Hurricane Melissa poses additional challenges. Area One consists of St. James, Hanover, Trelawny and Westmoreland. Acting Command Officer for Area One, Senior Superintendent Othniel Dobson, says additional resources from Kingston divisions will be seeing more police officers out in the town centres, out on the roads and the corridors, providing that enhanced security presence for us all. We also have additional resources in the form of other formations, branches coming out of Kingston, the Agricultural Protection Branch that deals with pre-elastic and other issues. They specialize operations, giving us additional strength in some of our areas, our BOPD and our military
