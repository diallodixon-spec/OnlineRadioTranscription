The Saturday Night Alternative offers the best in vintage music across various genres, from the 1950s through to the 1990s, interspersed with short historical data. The Spotlight Hour seeks to portray an artist, a mood or a particular genre by verbal and musical presentation. Join me or host Roy Black every Saturday between 6pm and 11pm for this unique show right here on Roots 96.1 FM as we continue this musical journey that links the past to the present time.
