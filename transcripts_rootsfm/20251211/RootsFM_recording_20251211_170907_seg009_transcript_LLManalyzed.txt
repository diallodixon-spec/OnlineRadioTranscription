Health authorities say 32 deaths recorded during and shortly after Hurricane Melissa are still under investigation to determine whether they are directly linked to the storm. The Ministry of Health says the cases involve individuals who died from injuries, medical complications or circumstances where the hurricane may have played a contributing role. Post storm assessments across several parishes revealed that some victims were affected by falling debris, flooded areas and or delays in getting medical care. The ministry says forensic teams are reviewing medical records, witness statements, and environmental conditions at the time of each incident. Officials say the review process is thorough to ensure accurate reporting, which will help guide future disaster response.
