20251225 123826 There were some shepherds taking care of their sheep in a nearby field Angels from the heaven appeared to them and told the shepherds that a savior was born Do not be afraid they said I bring you good news that will cause great joy for all the people Today in the town of David a savior has been born to you. He is the Messiah, the Lord. This will be a sign to you, baby, wrapped in a manger. Suddenly, a great company of the heavenly hosts appeared with the angel, praising God and saying, glory to God in the highest heaven, and on earth peace to those on whom this favor rests. When the shepherds had seen him they spread the word concerning what had been told to them about the child and all who heard it were amazed.
