The Jamaica Constabulary Force JCF is bringing joy, stability and renewed hope to children across Western and Central Jamaica who have been impacted by Hurricane Melissa through its Children's Advocacy, Recovery and Empowerment Care Initiative. Head of the Constabulary Communications Network, CCN, Senior Superintendent of Police, SSP Delegarik, says the project is designed to address the immediate and long-term needs of youngsters. We are very caught up in everything and trying to provide the basics, but our children, they too need help. So this program is being led by SISOKA and the CSSB.
