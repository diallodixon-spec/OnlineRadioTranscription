And finally, President Donald Trump on Thursday ordered the federal government to hasten marijuana reclassification to enable research into its medicinal uses. The president signed an executive order directing Attorney General Pam Bondi to expedite the completion of rescheduling cannabis from a Class 1 drug with no medicinal uses and high potential for abuse to a Class 3 drug that has medicinal uses. The President says polling data from a large majority in the US supports rescheduling marijuana. Changing marijuana to the Schedule 3 drug would place it among drugs that require prescriptions to obtain and use.
