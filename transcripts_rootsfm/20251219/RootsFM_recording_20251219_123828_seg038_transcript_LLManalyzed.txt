Several government buildings were destroyed, including courthouses, municipal offices, the tax office, and the hospital. Homes, schools, and churches were also damaged while storm surge left roads blocked and large sections of the town inaccessible in the days after the hurricane. In the short term, the focus will be on restoring essential services through temporary arrangements. These include reopening hospital services, setting up temporary government offices, and continuing cleanup operations to make the town fully navigable. Minister Green says a comprehensive study will then guide the permanent rebuilding process.
