Spectrum Management Authority presents Unity in Adi City with their staging of a community wellness fair this Friday December 12 and you guessed it RootsFM will be there all day to bring you the happenings. It will be a day of help and healing providing love for all in the Spectrum Management Authority's parking lot at 13 to 19 Harbour Street between 8 a.m. and 4 p.m. It will be a day of unification and upliftment celebrating resilience as a community amidst the challenges. There will be blood pressure screenings, eye tests and HIV testing provided by doctors and nurses from the University Hospital of the West Indies. There will also be an early childhood stimulation corner and a reading area for children provided by the Early Childhood Commission. As well as the distribution of 200, yes, 200 care packages.
