20251216 124130 The man has died following a single vehicle crash in Old Harbour, St Catherine. The deceased has been identified as Kenan Perry of Herring Hill Lane in the Old Harbour area. According to reports, the incident occurred about 4.40 Sunday morning along Church Penn Main Road. Perry was driving a Toyota Mark X motor car when he reportedly failed to negotiate a corner and collided with a utility light pole. He sustained severe injuries in the crash and the vehicle was extensively damaged. Perry was taken to the Spanish Town Hospital for treatment, where he was later pronounced dead. Police say investigations are continuing into the circumstances surrounding the crash, including speed and other possible contributing factors. The incident has again highlighted concerns about road safety, particularly during the early morning hours when visibility and driver fatigue can be major risks. Lawmen are once more urging motorists to exercise caution
