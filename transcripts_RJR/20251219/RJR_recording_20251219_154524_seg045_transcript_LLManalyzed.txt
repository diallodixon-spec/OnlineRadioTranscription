20251219 154524 Are you a registered charity clearing charitable items during the holiday and in the new year? Here are some reminders from the Jamaica Customs Agency to assist you with your clearance. Ensure you have a valid copy of the Certificate of Registration from the Department of Cooperatives and Friendly Societies, a letter of authorization, if applicable, the signed special declaration, the bill of lading or airway bill, your packing list, your invoice or receipts, a valid tax compliance certificate, and the relevant permit and licenses, if applicable. Also, ensure that shipments are consigned to the charity at the registered address and that donations for schools are consigned through the National Education Trust. For customs related queries, you may contact Jamaica customs at eight seven six nine two two five one four zero to eight or email public dot relations at jca dot gov dot jm.
