20260102 195132 Do you know what to do if you lose your motor vehicle registration plates? Well, here are the steps. Report the matter to the police immediately. Purchase lost or stolen motor vehicle document fee at any tax office. Visit any police station and present the fee for the police report. Obtain the relevant letter from the police. Visit the nearest tax office. Complete the MD01 form, which is the application for motor vehicle transactions. This must be accompanied by the police letter, vehicle documents, valid ID, example passport, driver's license or national ID. In the case where only one plate was lost, the additional plate should be submitted. Pay the relevant fees at the cashier.
