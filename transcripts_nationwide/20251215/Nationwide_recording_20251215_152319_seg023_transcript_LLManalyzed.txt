Prime Minister Dr. The Most Honorable Andrew Holness has taken to social media to reiterate the government's support for the return of entertainment activities, emphasizing that the administration is not opposed to events but requires promoters to ensure public safety and proper planning. The message comes amid a national recovery period following the recent Hurricane Melissa. In an Instagram video reel and accompanying post, the Prime Minister sought to dispel rumors circulating on social media that citizens were prohibited from hosting parties during the Christmas season, which he warned could undermine public order. He underlined that entertainment and recreation are important for helping people cope with challenges and returning communities to normalcy. While encouraging the return of events, Dr. Holness stressed that enjoyment must be balanced with responsibility and called on organizers to be conscientious and plan properly
