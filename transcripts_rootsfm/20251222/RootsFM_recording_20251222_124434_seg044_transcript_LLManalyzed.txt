While the Bank of Jamaica says the national payment platform itself is operating correctly, it has acknowledged that some financial institutions are facing internal processing challenges. As a result, several customers and businesses have reported delays in having funds credited to their accounts. The Bank of Jamaica is advising anyone affected to contact their financial institution directly for updates on specific transactions. The upgrade has previously been postponed from an earlier November deadline after some banks reported readiness issues, including disruptions caused by Hurricane Melissa.
