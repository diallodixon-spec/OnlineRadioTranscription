20251212 180705 As of December 1, 2025, the National Land Agency has distributed 9,577 certificates of title under the Systematic Land Registration Program, nearly 6,000 of them here in the beautiful Parish of St. Elizabeth. These numbers represent thousands of families whose financial prospects have fundamentally improved. This morning, an additional 722 Jamaicans across 34 communities, from Triall to Topill, from Rosehall to Burnt Savannah, will receive their long-awaited titles. For many, this title is the first piece of paper in generations that definitively states this land is yours. Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness. Commanding officer for the St. Elizabeth Police Division, Superintendent Coleridge Minto, says there has been a significant decline in major crimes in the division this year.
