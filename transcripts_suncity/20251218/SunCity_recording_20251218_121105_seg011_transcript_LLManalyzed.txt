The Guyana government has announced a $100,000 cash grant for all citizens age 18 and over to be included in the 2026 national budget as part of a slate of new economic initiatives unveiled by President Irfan Ali on Wednesday night. The president also announced monthly salaries of $50,000 for 10-day government workers, the creation of tax-free special development zones, abolition of net property tax for individuals, and expanded financing for small and medium enterprises through the Guyana Development Bank.
