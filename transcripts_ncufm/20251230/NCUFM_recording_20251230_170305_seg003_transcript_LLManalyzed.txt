20251230 170305 Over more than two decades, Donald Oliver built one of the most distinguished careers in local sports media, spanning radio, television, live commentary, production, and journalism. He worked with major media houses, including RJR Communications Group, CVM Television, Sportsmax, and most recently, the Jamaica Observer, where he served as audiovisual manager. Oliver became a household name through his iconic football commentary, covering major competitions such as the schoolboy football season, Jamaica Premier League, Reggae Boys matches, CONCACAF Nations League fixtures, and international friendlies. He also covered the Olympic Games, Commonwealth Games, and major international sporting events. The GFF notes that his vivid commentary style brought matches to life, captivating audiences with insight, passion, and professionalism. Oliver's work earned him numerous awards, including the Prime Minister's Youth Award for Excellence in Journalism.
