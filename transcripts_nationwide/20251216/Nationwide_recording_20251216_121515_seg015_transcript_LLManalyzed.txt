20251216 121515 One new JCF member has joined the contingent. Jamaica now has a total of 26 members of the security forces deployed to Haiti. This is the third Jamaican contingent to be deployed to the French-speaking Caribbean neighbor. The JDF says the planned rotation ensures the sustained effectiveness and operational readiness of Jamaica's deployed personnel while maintaining continuity of mission objectives. The previous two deployments supported the multinational security support mission, which preceded the GSF. The GSF works closely with the Haitian National Police and the Haitian Armed Forces to conduct operations aimed at restoring safety and security for the citizenry. It provides security for critical infrastructure and supporting humanitarian access. The Jamaican contingent provides operational, strategic and logistic support to the GSF headquarters.
