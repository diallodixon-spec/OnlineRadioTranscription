Legendary sprinter Usain Bolt, through his foundation, has partnered with the Janet Richards Foundation in a nearly $1 million donation to the Mustard Seed Blessed Assurance Children's Home in St. James. The presentation included patio furniture, a tent, a portable swimming pool, educational supplies, and hot meals, bringing joy to children and staff. Each staff member also received a gift package. Janet Richards thanked Bolt for his continued support, noting the donations valued at about $900,000, while plans are already in motions to add a new play area.
