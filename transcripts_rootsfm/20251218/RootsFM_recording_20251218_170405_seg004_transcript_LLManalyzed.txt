20251218 170405 Jamaica's push towards a fully digitized healthcare system received a major boost with the handover of U.S. $1.3 million worth of equipment to support the country's electronic immunization registry. Minister of Health and Wellness Dr. Christopher Tufton was on hand to receive the equipment during a handover ceremony held at the St. Anne Health Department in St. Anne on December 11. The EIR is a digital system designed to track all the vaccine doses administered to individuals and it replaces traditional paper records with a more efficient and reliable method of data collection and storage.
