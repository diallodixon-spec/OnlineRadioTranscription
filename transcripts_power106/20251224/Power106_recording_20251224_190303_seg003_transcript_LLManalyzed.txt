One recipient, Blondie Edwards, who led a very active life before losing one of her legs to illness, is grateful for the chance to regain her mobility and fully participate in life. The assistive aid grants provided through the Jamaica Council for Persons with Disabilities, JCPD, are valued at $300,000 each. However, the Ministry of Labour and Social Security has taken it a step further and will provide the balance needed for the prosthetic devices for all five beneficiaries.
