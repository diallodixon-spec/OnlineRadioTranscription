Dolphin Cove is one of Jamaica's top marine attractions, where visitors can swim with dolphins and interact with other sea creatures in their natural environment. The park offers thrilling activities like glass bottom kayaking, snorkeling with stingrays, mini boat rides and even swimming with and feeding sharks. With options ranging from gentle dolphin touch encounters to the immersive dolphin trek, it's a must visit for anyone wanting to explore the island's vibrant marine life.
