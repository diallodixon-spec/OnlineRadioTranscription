Police say the intersections of High Street and Exchange Drive and High Street and School Street will be closed from 8 a.m. on Wednesday. Access at both locations will be limited to pedestrians and authorized personnel only. Traffic at the High Street and Exchange Drive intersection will be diverted north along Exchange Drive, while traffic at High Street and School Street will be redirected north along School Street. Additionally, two-way traffic on Exchange Drive will be diverted onto Lovers Lane and Central Road, while traffic on School Street will be diverted on to Central Road and Lovers Lane. Motorists are being urged to obey police instructions and exercise caution in the area.
