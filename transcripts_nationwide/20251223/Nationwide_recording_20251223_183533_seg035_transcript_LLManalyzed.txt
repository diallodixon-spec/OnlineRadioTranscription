The Bank of Jamaica BOJ is temporarily waiving the automated clearing house ACH penalty for transactions equal to or exceeding $1 million. It means that transactions exceeding the threshold may be conducted via ACH without attracting the $5,000 fee. The waiver period is December 15 to 31, 2025. BOJ says the move is in response to the challenges being experienced by some commercial banks, with crediting of real-time gross settlement, RTGS, payments and transfers to beneficiaries beneficiaries accounts.
