In a notice issued to the public and interest groups yesterday, the minister said the move follows an application by the concessionaires on the Section 23 of the Toll Roads Act. The minister has indicated his intention to make an order allowing for the levy of tolls on that stretch of roadway. The proposed rates for motorists traveling from Maypen to Williamsfield are as follows. For Class 1 vehicles, the cost will be $480, Class 2, $720, and Class 3 vehicles, $1,400. For motorists using tollgate access ramp, the proposed rates are $240 for Class 1 vehicles, $360 for Class 2, and $700 for Class 3 vehicles.
