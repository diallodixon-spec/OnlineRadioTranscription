More than 600 entities across Triloni, Hanover, St. Elizabeth and St. James have so far received direct assistance. The support program includes the reconstruction of a cluster of 44 small shops in border St. Elizabeth, built to be more hurricane resilient and flood protected. Grocery operations and cold storage capacity in White House Westmoreland have also been restored through targeted generator support. State Minister Delano Siv writes as while the reinstatement of electricity, water and telecommunications services has progressed steadily, many small and micro enterprises, particularly in Western Jamaica, continue to face disruption, making targeted business recovery support a priority. He noted that the focus is on speed, coordination and practical interventions to help businesses reopen and preserve jobs.
