Residents of St. Elizabeth enjoyed a brief moment of relief from the hardships brought on by Hurricane Melissa with a New Year's fireworks display in Black River. The event, staged by the Urban Development Corporation, brought moments of joy to families still struggling to recover from the storm's destruction. Among them is DeLarine Morgan of Fivespen, who says the celebration provided temporary relief from the stress of rebuilding her life. Ms. Morgan is still without electricity and is currently living under tarpaulin after her home was damaged by the hurricane.
