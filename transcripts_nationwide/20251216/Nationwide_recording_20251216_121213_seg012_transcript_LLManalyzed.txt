Head of the St. Elizabeth Police Division, Superintendent Coleridge Minto, says the parish has maintained the lowest murder tally in 25 years. Murders are down 47% in the parish. Between January 1 and December 13, 2025, 16 people were killed in St. Elizabeth. The figure stood at 30 during the same period last year.
