Still in the news, the Urban Development Corporation, UDC, says it will expand its New Year's Eve fireworks display to five parishes to uplift the spirits of Jamaicans in Western Jamaica in the aftermath of Hurricane Melissa. In a release on Thursday, the UDC explained that in addition to the traditional shows in downtown Kingston, Ocho Rios, and Harmony Beach Park in Montego Bay, it will bring New Year's fireworks to Black River St. Elizabeth and Long Bay Beach Negril. It says the wider staging is intended to give more families access to a meaningful end-of-year experience as the country continues on its path toward recovery. The UDC says while the celebrations will take a more modest approach this year, it's important to create opportunities for joy, reflection and community spirit as Jamaicans look toward 2026 with hope.
