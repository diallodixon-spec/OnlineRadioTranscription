20251229 195034 today. The state-run Jamaica Urban Transit Company, JUTC, is now providing regular commuter service between Ocho Rios and Kingston for a bus fare as low as $500 for an adult one-way. That's right, $500. Keep in mind that commuters traveling on public passenger vehicles between Ocho Reos and Kingston can pay between $700 and $1,000 for the journey on each leg. The value-packed Route 610 bus ride offers safe, professional, fixed schedules that allow commuters to plan their travel with the assurance of a reliable return. Corporate Communications Manager at the JUTC, Shantul Thompson, says the former express route was discontinued and regular service implemented based on commuter feedback.
