They built and equipped a 6,000 square feet factory at Twytham Park in St. Catherine. 1965 was the official birth of Wsinko. The new plant started production and manufactured 60 pairs of boots per hour. During that same time, Andrew and William Mufood were born. Wasinko Group Limited was formed as a result of the amalgamation of the West Indies Synthetics Limited, Wasinca Trading Limited, and Jamaica Drink Company Limited in 2006. In 2013, Joseph Mahfood retired as chairman and Andrew Mahfood was appointed CEO. William Mahfood was appointed the new chairman.
