Mr. McKenzie adds that elderly persons will have the option of being housed and taken care of at the parish infirmary and support will be provided for persons who can be accommodated by relatives or friends. Persons whose houses were destroyed will receive assistance and a temporary housing site will be established on the nearby playing field for remaining residents. And the government has introduced a new requirement for procuring entities to publicly disclose contracts awarded in situations of emergency or extreme urgency on the government of Jamaica Electronic Procurement System, GOGEP. This new requirement reinforces the government's commitment to transparency, accountability, and public oversight. In keeping with the Public Procurement Amendment Act 2025, all procuring entities are now required to publish emergency contract award notices within one month of the date of award.
