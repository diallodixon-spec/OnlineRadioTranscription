The fisherman accused of killing three members of his family at their home in Rocky Point last week was remanded yesterday when he appeared in the Clarendon Parish Court. Henry is accused of killing his mother, Annette Lindo, his brother, 32-year-old fisherman Linville Henry, otherwise called Bebe, and his cousin Keisha Lindo.
