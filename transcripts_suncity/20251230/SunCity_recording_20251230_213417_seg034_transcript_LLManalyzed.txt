For the past three years, the school has been actively participating in an annual beach clean-up activity, transforming environmental stewardship into hands-on actionable action. Nestled along the coastline of the small fishing village of Robbins Bay, the school overlooks what grade 6 teacher Alicia Wiley describes as a perfect view of the beach. Now, with the sea just steps away from their daily view, she says educating students about the environment and the importance of protecting it is not optional but necessary.
