Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness is encouraging greater private sector participation in the national recovery effort following Hurricane Melissa. He made the call while speaking during Thursday's presentation of building supplies, valued at $50 million, to the Office of Disaster Preparedness and Emergency Management by Chemtech Development and Construction. The contribution is to support the rebuilding of roofs and homes across parishes most severely impacted by the hurricane, strengthening national recovery efforts, and providing relief to affected families. Prime Minister Holness commended the company and other corporate entities that have already extended support to vulnerable communities and individuals severely impacted by the recent hurricane.
