The Caribbean Development Bank is offering a grant of $190,000 to support regional events and conferences aimed at strengthening the Caribbean's cultural and creative industries. The funding is being made available through the Cultural and Creative Industry Innovation Fund, with application closing on January 31st next year. The CDB says six grants ranging from $20,000 to $50,000 will be awarded for events scheduled between March 2026 and February 2027, focusing on policy dialogue and industry development.
