20251217 130204 The Transport Minister has issued a request for submissions to proposed toll rates for the Maypen to Williamsfield leg of Highway 2000. In a notice posted to the public and interested groups yesterday, the Minister said the move allows the application by a concessionaire under Section 23 of the Toll Roads Act. The Minister has indicated his intention to make an order allowing for the levy of tolls on that stretch of roadway. The proposed rates for motorists traveling from Maypen to Williamsfield are for Class 1 vehicles, $480. Class 2, $720. Class 3, $1,400. For motorists using the tollgate access ramp, the rates proposed are $240 for Class 1, $360 for Class 2, and $700 for Class 3 vehicles.
