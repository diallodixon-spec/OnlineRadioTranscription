In related news, the Firearm Licensing Authority, FLA, is reminding all licensed firearm holders that gun salutes are strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated under any circumstances. In a release on Wednesday, FLA emphasized that gun salutes pose a serious risk to public safety, resulting in injuries and fatalities. It says any discharge of a licensed firearm outside of lawful purposes reaches the terms and conditions of that firearm license and the Firearms Act carrying a penalty of up to three years imprisonment or fine
