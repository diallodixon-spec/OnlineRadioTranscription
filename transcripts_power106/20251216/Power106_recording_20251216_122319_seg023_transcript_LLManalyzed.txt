The Ministry of Labor and Social Security has deployed specialized teams to ward against any practice of child labor during the post-Hurricane recovery phase. Portfolio Minister Perno Child Jr. says the ministry is maintaining a strong presence in affected communities through ongoing assessments and is paying close attention to the risk of child labor. We have a team here that is focused on eradicating child labor because in this restoration process, we know that there will be the risk of any of our children being unfortunately and inadvertently used as part of the process. We're noticing any of the kids on the roof doing anything too dangerous. Labor and Social Security Minister Pernal Charles Jr. speaking during the Prime Minister's Christmas treat at the Petersfield High School shelter in Westmoreland on December 13. Minister Charles added that officers from the ministry's labor division are actively engaging individuals to help them safely transition back into economic activity.
