Flu jabs will be available on Christmas Day in Workington in a bid to reduce the risk of passing on the virus to family members over the festive period. Dr. Muna Hen says flu has started to circulate five weeks earlier than usual. She says those 85 and over are mostly the group of people who are becoming unwell. She says the spread of the virus over the Christmas period was a concern as children mix with more family members.
