20251226 150102 As we recover and rebuild after the hurricane, it's important to keep your medication safe and use them as prescribed. Here are some quick medication safety tips from the NHF. Use bottled or cooled boiled water when taking your meds. If they get wet or damaged, do not take them. Store them in a cool, dry space away from sunlight. If your medication needs refrigeration, talk to your pharmacist about the best way to keep it.
