This year's IDEVA theme, Unite to End Digital Violence Against All Women and Girls, speaks to an era where our work is rapidly evolving. Technology has created new forms of harm, cyber bullying, online harassment and the non-consensual sharing of intimate images. Even so, Jamaica is strengthening its legislative framework, building partnerships with digital platforms and expanding training. Minister Grange also acknowledged the ongoing recovery efforts from Hurricane Melissa noting that the ministry through its bureau continues to support psychosocial care, counseling and access to safe spaces for women and girls who face heightened risks after natural disasters.
