20251216 092016 God provides, God appoints, God sustains. And as leadership shifts, his heart remains the same. The shepherd may change, but the shepherd of shepherds, or Lord, he remains. And he is faithful to give us leaders who will guide with knowledge, with understanding, and with his heart. As one voice passes the baton and another takes it up, we rest assured God himself is the giver of shepherds and his promise of guidance will never fail. And that was our personal promise from the word. We are claiming God's promises over or whole lives.
