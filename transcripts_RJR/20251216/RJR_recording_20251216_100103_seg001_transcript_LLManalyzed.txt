Economic Affairs, Entrepreneurship and Technological Innovation Minister Andrew Barserand has said Suriname is likely to host an international United Nations Industrial Development Organisation, UNIDO, to promote Suriname 2.0, the vision for the next 50 years. He said the collaboration is intended to boost not only trade and tourism, but also the country's factories and industry, with the goal of becoming a regional leader. Mr. Basaran's statement comes as President Jennifer Greenling-Simmons has given her blessing for the launch of the Country Partnership Programme with UNIDO, marking a significant step for the government toward economic diversification and sustainable industrial development.
