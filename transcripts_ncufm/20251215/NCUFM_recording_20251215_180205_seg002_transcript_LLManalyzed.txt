Moving over to athletics, as organizers of the 2028 Olympic Games in Los Angeles have announced that at least one million tickets will be priced at 28 US dollars. The move is aimed at improving accessibility, as fans will be able to register their interest from next month. A random ticket draw covering all Olympic sports opens on January 14, with successful applicants given time slots to purchase tickets in April. The announcement comes amid growing criticism of rising ticket prices for major global sporting events, particularly the 2026 FIFA World Cup across the United States, Canada and Mexico.
