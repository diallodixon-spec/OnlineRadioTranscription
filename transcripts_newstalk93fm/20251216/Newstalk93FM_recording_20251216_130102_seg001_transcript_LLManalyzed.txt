The Rural Agricultural Development Authority and the Agricultural Land Management Division have collaborated to conduct soil tests for farmers in hurricane-affected areas, part of ongoing replanting supportive measures to ensure farmers maximize output. The Ministry of Health and Wellness, in collaboration with the Inter-American Development Bank, will be conducting an ecological feasibility study of all hospitals located in coastal areas, with the findings expected to guide the government's decision on potential relocation.
