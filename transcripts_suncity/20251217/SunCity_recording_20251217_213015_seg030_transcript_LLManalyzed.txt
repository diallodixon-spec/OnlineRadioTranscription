20251217 213015 Christmas is generally a time of joy, laughter, celebration, and togetherness. But how can persons devastated by Hurricane Melissa find hope and happiness this Christmas? Stay tuned as psychotherapist Adélie Fraser shares strategies on how to navigate the holiday season and manage your mental health. With only days away from Christmas and festivities in some quarters in full swing, the reality is that Christmas does not put a stop on people's suffering, whether at the hands of Hurricane Melissa or financial strain. There are, however, helpful strategies that persons can apply to better manage their mental state and find hope. Psychotherapist Adélie Fraser suggests that taking a moment to check in with yourself is an important first step.
