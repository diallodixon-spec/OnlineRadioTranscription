India scored a third to run win over South Africa in the fifth and final T20 International to wrap up the series 3-1 in a meta-bat on Friday. Sent in to bat, India posted a challenging 231 for 5 from their 20 overs. Tilak Varma top-scored with 73 from just 42 balls, which included 10 4s and 1 6. He was supported by Man of the Match Hardik Pandya, who blasted 63 from just 25 balls with 5 6s and 5 4s. South Africa's chase was led by Omna Quinton de Kock but they fell short at two hundred and one for eight. De Kock top-scored with 65 from 35 in the losing effort.
