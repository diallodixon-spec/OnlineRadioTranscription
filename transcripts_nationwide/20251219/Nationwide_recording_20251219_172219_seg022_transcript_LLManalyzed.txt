In Guyana, the government says it will provide a $100,000 cash grant to every Guyanese aged 18 years and older. The funds will be allocated under its 2026 national budget. President Dr. Irfan Ali made the announcement while outlining his administration's economic and social agenda for the next five years. Beyond the universal cash grant, President Ali says the government will also roll out direct assistance to households to support the construction and upgrading of homes. Parents of school-aged children are also set to benefit from cash grants.
