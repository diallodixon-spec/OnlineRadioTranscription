2025-12-12 11:01:26 to get started today. Are you a registered charity clearing charitable items during the holiday and in the new year? Here are some reminders from the Jamaica Customs Agency to assist you with your clearance. Ensure you have a valid copy of the certificate of registration from the Department of Cooperative and Friendly Societies, a letter of authorization if applicable, the signed special declaration, the bill of lading or airway bill, your packing list, your invoice or receipts, a valid tax compliance certificate, and the relevant permit and licenses, where applicable. Also ensure that shipments are consigned to the charity at the registered address and that donations for schools are consigned through the National Education Trust. For customs related queries, you may contact Jamaica Customs at 876-922-5140 to 8 or email public dot relations at jca.gov.jm. Jamaica Customs, keeping our customers in focus.
