The West Maloney Traffic Department is investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident involving two young boys who were struck from their bicycle by a vehicle driven by a member of the Jamaica Defense Force in Cornwall Mountain on Tuesday. The incident occurred sometime after 3 p.m. It's understood that the boys, age nine and five, were riding a bicycle along the main road when the driver of a green Toyota Land Cruiser motor truck traveling in the opposite direction failed to negotiate a corner, overturned, and fell on the boys. Reports received by our news center on Tuesday is that one of the boys was knocked unconscious. He suffered injuries to his head, chest, feet, mouth and back.
