20251223 121820 national news now, regional news. Venezuela has shored up support from China and Russia as it confronts a U.S. blockade of sanctioned oil tankers. This as Donald Trump continues to ramp up his campaign of pressure on the South American country's president, Nicolas Maduro. China's foreign ministry said on Monday that the seizure of another country's ships were or was a serious violation of international law after the US intercepted a China-bound all-tanker of the Venezuelan coast on Saturday. The US targeted a third tanker on Sunday. China is the biggest buyer of Venezuelan crude, which accounts for roughly 4% of its oil imports. The foreign ministers of Russia also criticized the US sanctions, which have included strikes on alleged drug trafficking boats. A statement from the Russian Foreign Ministry reaffirmed its full support for and solidarity with the Venezuelan leadership and people
