A 33-year-old woman has been charged with obtaining money by means of false pretence arising from an incident in Spanish town between Thursday, December 4 and Monday, December 8. She has been identified as Janice Linton of Waterford Portmore in St Catherine. Reports from the Portmore police are that during the mentioned period, Linton advertised a property for rental using an online platform. She later received $75,000 from a woman as a down payment for the unit. It was further reported that Linton gave the woman a key for the house, however the property could not be located. A report was made to the police and following an investigation, Linton was taken into custody during an operation on Wednesday December 24.
