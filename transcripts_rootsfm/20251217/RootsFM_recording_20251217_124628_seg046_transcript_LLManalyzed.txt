20251217 124628 The section of the Spanish Town Road near the Sandy Gully Bridge in Kingston will be temporarily closed to vehicular traffic to facilitate emergency sewer repairs. The National Works Agency says the closure, which began at 9pm last night, will run nightly from 9pm to 5am. The road is expected to fully reopen by 5am tomorrow morning. According to the NWA, the temporary closure is necessary to repair a leaking sewer line in the area. The agency says the work is being carried out during overnight hours to minimize disruption to daytime traffic flow along the busy corridor. Stephen Shaw, manager of communication and customer services at the NWA, explained that traffic arrangements will be adjusted while the repairs are ongoing.
