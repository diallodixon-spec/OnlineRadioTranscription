St Vincent and the Grenadines is among a handful of caricom countries that enjoy diplomatic relations with Taiwan, which China regards as a renegade province and has insisted that all countries adhere to its One China policy. In August 2016, then-NDP president and opposition leader Arnim Youstas announced that if elected to office, the NDP would switch diplomatic recognition to China. Prime Minister Dr. Godwin Friday, who replaced Mr. Youstas as NDP president in November 2017, has not announced any change in the party's position, even as it has repeatedly hinted that it would maintain the diplomatic ties.
