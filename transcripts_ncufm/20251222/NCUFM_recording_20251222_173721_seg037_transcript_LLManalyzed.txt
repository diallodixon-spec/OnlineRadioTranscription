So what are some signs to look for when the battery might be failing? Well keep an eye and also an ear for these red flags. You might have a slow crank. The engine takes a bit longer to start. Your headlights might seem a bit dimmer as well because headlights appear yellow or dim especially at idle when the battery is failing.
