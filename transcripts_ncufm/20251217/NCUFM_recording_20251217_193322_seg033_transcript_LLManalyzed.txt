The Ministry of Industry, Investment and Commerce is providing coordinated support to micro, small and medium-sized enterprises, MSMEs, as part of the government's ongoing economic recovery efforts following the impact of Hurricane Melissa. State Minister in the Ministry, Delano Sibbright, says although the restoration of electricity, water and telecommunications services is progressing steadily, many small and micro enterprises continue to face disruptions. As a result, targeted business recovery support has become a priority.
