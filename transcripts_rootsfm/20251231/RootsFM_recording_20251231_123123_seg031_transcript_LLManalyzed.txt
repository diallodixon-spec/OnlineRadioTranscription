20251231 123123 Police are investigating the fatal shooting of a taxi operator in Bowers District, St Catherine, early this morning. The victim has been identified as 56-year-old Rudolf Francis, otherwise called Rusty of St Catherine. Reports are that shortly after 12.40 this morning, loud explosions were heard in the area. Francis was later found lying on the roadway suffering from gunshot wounds. The police were summoned and the scene was processed. Francis was taken to hospital where he was pronounced dead. No motive has so far been established for the killing and investigators from the old Harbor Criminal Investigation Branch are leading the probe and are appealing to anyone with information to come forward.
