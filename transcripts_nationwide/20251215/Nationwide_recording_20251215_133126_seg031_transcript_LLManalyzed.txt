20251215 133126 The Police Civilian Oversight Authority will be hosting its Transforming Our Police Service TOPS program on Thursday, December 18, 2025. The TOPS program, established in 2019, aims at ensuring that police officers adhere to Jamaica Constabulary Force policies. Additionally, the program looks at boosting their confidence, morale, and spirits. The program is comprised of four phases. The TOPS Area Competition, the TOPS Area Award Ceremony, Area Command Performance and Area Command Reinspection, all phases that foster professionalism and accountability. Join Cliff Hughes online from 10am to 1pm as we broadcast live from the TOPS program on Thursday, December 18, 2025, only on Nationwide 90FM.
