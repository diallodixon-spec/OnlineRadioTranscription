In business news, the Bank of Jamaica reports that net remittance flows, the difference between money sent home by Jamaicans overseas and funds sent out of the country, amounted to US $1.65 billion between April 1st and December 13th this year. The figure is US $44.3 million, or just over 2 percent higher than the amount recorded over the same period last year.
