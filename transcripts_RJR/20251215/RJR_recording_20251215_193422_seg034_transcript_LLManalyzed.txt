And Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holness has sought to reassure Jamaicans that there is no ban on festive celebrations this holiday season. Speaking at a Christmas treat for shelteries at Petersfield High School in Westmoreland recently, Dr. Holness emphasized that once the events are properly organized and align with public safety requirements, there should be no objections. He also advised that promoters looking to stage events in communities still facing infrastructural challenges after Hurricane Melissa must suggest several practical considerations. The police have said that they will give permits for parties so long as the promoters make the proper arrangements.
