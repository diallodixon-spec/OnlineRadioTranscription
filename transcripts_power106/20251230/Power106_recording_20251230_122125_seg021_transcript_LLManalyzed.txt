Commissioner of Police, Dr. Kevin Blake, is reporting that the number of fatal shootings by the police this year represents a small fraction of the interactions between the police and civilians. Delivering his end-of-year review to the nation recently, Dr. Blake acknowledged that there has been considerable public discussion surrounding police fatal shootings this year. He further outlined that there were over 100,000 interactions between the police and members of the public, with only 0.3% of them resulting in a fatal shooting by police officers. While we are not happy with the increased number of police fatal shootings, the number represents less than 0.3% of all police-citizen interactions. Our focus deterrence strategy aims at throwing significant amount of resources around the highest risk targets. These are persons who have made a life of killing people.
