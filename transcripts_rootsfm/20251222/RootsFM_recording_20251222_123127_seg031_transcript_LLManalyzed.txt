Five men have been arrested and charged following the seizure of three illegal firearms and more than 100 rounds of ammunition during a series of police operations carried out between November 11th and December 19th. Two of the arrests stemmed from joint operations involving the Firearms and Narcotics Investigation Division and the Jamaica Customs Agency at Port Bustamante in Kingston. During one of the operations, officers seized a Smith and Wesson revolver, along with 50 rounds of ammunition from a shipment, leading to the arrest of 60-year-old Roger Shakes, a dual-citizen of the United States and Jamaica.
