Commissioner of Police Dr Kevin Blake is reporting that the number of fatal shootings by the police this year represents a small fraction of the interactions between the police and civilians. Delivering his end-of-year review to the nation recently, Dr Blake acknowledged that there has been considerable public discussion surrounding police fatal shootings this year. He further outlined that there were over 100,000 interactions between the police and members of the public, with only 0.3% of all police-citizen interactions. Our focus deterrence strategy aims at throwing significant amount of resources around the highest risk targets. These are persons who have made a life of killing people. In spite of this, we have and will continue to appeal to these individuals not to meet us with violence.
