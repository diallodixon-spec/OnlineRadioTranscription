20251229 091915 Every day is an opportunity to grow closer to God, to grow in love for others, and to grow in wisdom and strength. Think about it for a second. A single conversation can plant a seed of hope. A single act of kindness can change someone's day. A single prayer can shift the atmosphere of your home. That's the power of time when it's surrendered to God. But here's the challenge. The days are evil. Distractions, temptations, and worries are always competing for our attention. If we're not careful, time slips away into things that don't build us up. That's why scripture calls us to be intentional, to redeem the time, to make it count.
