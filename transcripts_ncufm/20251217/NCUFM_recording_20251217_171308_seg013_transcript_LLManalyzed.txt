20251217 171308 recovery work continues island-wide. In regional news, public sector workers and pensioners in St. Kitts and Nevis will receive a double salary ahead of the Christmas season. That's according to Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Dr. Terrence Drew. Speaking during the 2026 budget address, Dr. Drew announced that an additional payment equal to one month's salary will be paid this Friday. The benefit will apply to civil servants, government auxiliary employees, pensioners and step workers under both the federal government and the Nevis Island administration. The Prime Minister said the decision recognizes the dedication, service and continued contribution of public employees and retirees to national development.
