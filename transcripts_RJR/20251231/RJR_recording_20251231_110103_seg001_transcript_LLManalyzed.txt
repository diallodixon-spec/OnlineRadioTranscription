Despite assurances from the National Water Commission that water would be trucked to communities in Santa Cruz, St. Elizabeth, residents say they have received no such service. Residents of Beadles Drive, Providence Gardens, Beadles Heights and the Town Centre say they have been without regular water supply since the passage of Hurricane Melissa. Business operators are also affected, saying they have been forced to purchase water, leading in some cases to the closure of public sanitary conveniences. The affected residents are appealing to the NWC to honour its commitment to provide water. Efforts to obtain a comment from the NWC have so far been unsuccessful.
