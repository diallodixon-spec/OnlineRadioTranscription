In related news, JPS restored power to the town of Black River in St. Elizabeth before sunrise today, delivering on its promise ahead of time. JPS president and CEO Hugh Grant had yesterday promised that the town would have electricity by Christmas night. Travel tour agency Sainti876 shared a photo online of JPS crew members and their vehicles under a street light illuminated, showing that light has returned along the town's main lines. The photo also featured a time stamp revealing that residents received light sometime around 5.44am. In release, JPS informed that the Black River Hospital is now energized, becoming the last major hospital to receive electricity since Hurricane Melissa hit the island. The electricity company says Black River is the last parish capital to receive power, following the Category 5 storm which destroyed the transmission network.
