20251215 172018 Student Loan Bureau's recently launched Debt Reset Program. The program was launched on December 3. It's one of the government's manifesto promises announced earlier this year. The initiative aims to significantly reduce the prepayment amount for debtors. In an update on Monday, the SLB says 3,600 clients have applied for the program. SLB is offering a limited time initiative to assist borrowers with targeted loan accounts that are in arrears as at October 31. Eligible persons will receive a 100% waiver of late fees and insurance charges and a 50% waiver of interest arrears. Additionally, a one-time credit of up to $100,000 will be made to accounts in good standing. The SLB says 800 people have since sought the arrears credit, while 2,900 have applied for the good standing credit.
