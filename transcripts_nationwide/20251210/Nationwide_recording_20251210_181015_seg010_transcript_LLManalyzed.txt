The Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining wishes to announce that an Animal Tagging Blitz will take place in Manchester on Saturday December 13, commencing at 8am. The targeted communities include Rose Hill, Rudds Corner, Crosskeys and Porous. Farmers who have cattle requiring tagging are invited to contact the National Animal Identification and Traceability System on 876-648-5672 or the Veterinary Services Division by telephone at 876-977-2489 or 876-977-2492 or by email at vsd at moa.gov.jm
