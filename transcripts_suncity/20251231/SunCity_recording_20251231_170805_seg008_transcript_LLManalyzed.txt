20251231 170805 More in our local news. Members of the public are being reminded that parking along the Palisades Main Road in Kingston to view fireworks is illegal and strictly prohibited. The police say no vehicles will be allowed to park on the roadway and the parking will also be banned on the right-hand side of the road heading towards the Norman Manley International Airport between 5 p.m. Wednesday and 1230 a.m. Thursday. Motorists found in breach of the restriction will be prosecuted and their vehicles will be towed. The police are urging road users to comply with the regulations to ensure public safety. Finally, in local news, the Firearm Licensing Authority and the Jamaica Constabulary Force are warning the public against the illegal practice of celebratory gun salutes during New Year celebrations.
