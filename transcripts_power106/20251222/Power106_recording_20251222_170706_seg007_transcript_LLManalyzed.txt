20251222 170706 The Clarendon fisherman accused of killing three members of his family at their home in Rocky Point last week has been charged. Daniel Henry was charged with three counts of murder on the weekend by detectives attached to the Major Investigation Division. He's accused of killing his mother, Annette Lindo, his brother, 32-year-old fisherman Linval Henry, otherwise called Bebe, and his cousin Keisha Lindo. He was remanded when he appeared in court today. Investigators had reported that about 9, 10 p.m. last Thursday, Henry and his brother Linville were at home in Fraser's Field when a dispute developed between them. Henry reportedly chopped his brother to the neck, killing him on the spot. The police say their mother, Annette Lindo, who ran into the yard calling for help, was Lindo's cousin, Keisha, inquired what was going on when she was attacked by the accused and fatally chopped. The body of Annette Lindo was later discovered inside the house with chop wounds.
