And approximately 200 farmers and fisher folk in Treasure Beach, St. Elizabeth have received over $7 million in supplies and equipment as part of Hurricane Melissa recovery activities. The supplies were provided by the National Commercial Bank Foundation in partnership with the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining. The initiative provided about 100 farmers with vouchers valued at $50,000 each for fertilizer, string trimmers or weed-whackers, sprays and other tools. Another 100 fishermen received fish pots, wires, ropes and floats. Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining Floyd Green says the initiative formed part of a coordinated national recovery effort to accelerate rebuilding in affected communities.
