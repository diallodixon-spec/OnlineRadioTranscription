More than 400 homeless people were treated to a bit of Christmas cheer yesterday during the Kingston and St. Andrew Municipal Corporation's annual Christmas Day feeding program. Those in attendance were served a meal and given gifts at Water Lane in downtown Kingston. Chief Executive Officer of the Kingston and St Andrew Municipal Corporation, Robert Hill, is reiterating the appeal for charitable groups and individuals to follow the procedure for feeding the homeless. Mr. Hill says the program, facilitated through the Poor Relief Department, will eliminate the disorder that currently exists.
