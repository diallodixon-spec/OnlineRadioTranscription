20251215 122322 Preceding Christmas Safety Tip is brought to you by the Firearm Licensing Authority. Planning to travel overseas for the holidays? Always ensure that your firearm is properly stored before you leave. Licensed firearm holders must leave their firearms at the Firearms Licensing Authority, FLA or the nearest police station prior to departure. Never leave your firearm unattended at home or susceptible to theft. Taking this responsible step ensures your safety and the safety of others while also complying with the law.
