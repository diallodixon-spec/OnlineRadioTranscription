Electricity has been restored to sections of Treasure Beach surrounding communities in St. Elizabeth with the installation of an emergency mobile power generation unit by the Jamaica Public Service. The temporary unit was officially launched this week and is now supplying electricity to just under 1,000 homes as recovery efforts continue following the passage of Hurricane Melissa. The unit was installed at Jack Spratt Lawn and is intended to provide relief while permanent transmission infrastructure is rebuilt. President and Chief Executive Officer of JPS Hugh Grant says the damage caused by the hurricane made it clear that full restoration would take time.
