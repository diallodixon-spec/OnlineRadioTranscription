Attention motorists, toll rates will be introduced at the toll plazas on the May Penn to Williams Field Highway on Saturday, December 27, 2025. For a full listing of these toll rates, please visit the Trans Jam Highway website at www.transjamhighways.com or their Instagram or Facebook pages using the handle at Trans Jam Highways. Please note that all these toll plazas and toll booth barriers will become fully operational at this time and motorists must slow down to 50 kilometers per hour or less as they approach the toll booths.
