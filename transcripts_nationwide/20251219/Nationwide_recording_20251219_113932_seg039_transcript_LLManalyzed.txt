The Ministry of Agriculture Fisheries and Mining wishes to announce that an animal tagging blitz will take place in Clarendon on Saturday, December 20, commencing at 8 a.m. The targeted communities include Vare Plains, comprising Lionel Town, Racecourse and Alley, Rhymesbury, Gravel Hill, Boys' Content, Yorktown, Denby Crawl, Racetrack, Lovelane and Crofts Hill. Farmers who have cattle requiring tagging are invited to contact the National Animal Identification and Traceability System on 876-648-5672, or the Veterinary Services Division by telephone on 876-977-2489, or
