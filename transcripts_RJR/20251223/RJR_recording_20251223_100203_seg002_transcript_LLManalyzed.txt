Bermuda Youth Social Development and Seniors Minister Tinei Furbert says a new report on dementia in Bermuda is intended to bring clarity, coordination and compassion to a system that has too often left families feeling overwhelmed and alone. She said dementia affects not only those living with the condition but also the families and caregivers who support them every day. The ministry in collaboration with the Bermuda Health Council and the Ministry of Health released the Dementia Integrated Care Pathway Report 2025 which provides a comprehensive evidence-based framework to improve how dementia is prevented, identified, treated and supported across Bermuda.
