Assistant Commissioner of Police assigned to the Strategic Operations portfolio and the Public Safety and Traffic Enforcement branch, Dr. Gary McKenzie, says the police will be clamping down on motorists who block other persons from moving freely. He says officers will be placed at strategic locations to ensure that motorists obey speed limits and also to ensure that vehicles are fit to operate on the roadways. We will not tolerate overloading. We will be checking vehicles to ensure that they are not defective. These things will be done in concert with the Island Traffic Authority and the Transport Authority. We have to ensure that safety prevails.
