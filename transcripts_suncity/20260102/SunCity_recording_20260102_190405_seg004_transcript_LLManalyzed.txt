Contributors are being reminded to ensure all information entered is accurate, particularly names, banking details, and contribution records, as errors can delay processing or require applications to be resubmitted. Applicants must also have their taxpayer registration number, TRN, at national identification system cards available when applying. The NHT also advises that the service standard for processing correctly submitted applications is 15 working days and warns contributors not to submit multiple applications unless advised by an NHT representative. The NHT is also urging contributors to be vigilant, noting an increase in fraudulent messages during the refund period. Customers are advised to avoid suspicious emails and links and to rely only on official NHT communication channels.
