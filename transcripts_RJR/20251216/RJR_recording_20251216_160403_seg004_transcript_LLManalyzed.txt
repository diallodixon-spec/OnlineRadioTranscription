20251216 160403 The National Housing Trust has an important disaster relief advisory, NHT contributors who suffered damage to property following the passage of Hurricane Melissa may apply for a hurricane relief grant of up to $500,000 to repair their homes or the homes of immediate family members. Application forms are available at NHT's office or via the website at nht.gov.jm. For more information, visit their website, contact your nearest NHT
