20251216 123528 Bellevue Hospital is urging families to visit relatives who are admitted at the facility and to take home those who have been discharged and are ready to return to their families for the Christmas season. In a statement issued on Monday, the hospital reminded Jamaicans that recovery is often strongest when patients are supported by family. While many households prepare for Christmas gatherings, hospital officials say several patients spend the season without any visits, phone calls or reassurance from loved ones. The hospital noted that some patients are clinically stable and fully discharged but remain at Bellevue because no family member has come forward to take them home.
