The opposition spokesman says the decision to abandon the Ocho Rios Express route confirms concerns that the rural express services were poorly planned and financially unsustainable with reports of buses operating with just 10 passengers. Phillips is now calling on Minister Vaz to release performance reports for the rural services and to ensure taxpayers are not footing the bill for what he describes as a failed transport experiment.
