The donated items included fertilizer, buckets, water boots, water pumps and wheelbarrows, which were distributed to approximately 120 farmers across Kingston, St. Andrew, St. Thomas and Portland. Chief Technical Director of Technical Services in the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining, Orville Palmer, applauded the JAS for assisting coffee farmers while also committing the support of the agriculture ministry in rebuilding the coffee sector. The provision of fertilizers to coffee farmers in collaboration with the Jamaica Coffee Growers Association directly supports recovery in the field and assist farmers as they work to restore productivity. The donation of small tools to food for the poor further support farmers as they return to active production.
