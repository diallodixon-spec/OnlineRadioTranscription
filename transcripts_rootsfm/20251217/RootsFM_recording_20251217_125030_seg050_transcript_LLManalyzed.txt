20251217 125030 The British Broadcasting Corporation says it will defend itself against a multi-billion dollar defamation lawsuit filed by United States President Donald Trump over an edited clip of his January 6th, 2021 speech used in a Panorama documentary. The lawsuit, filed in Florida, accuses the BBC of defamation and breaches of trade practices laws. Trump's legal team claims the broadcaster intentionally altered his speech to make it appear as though he made a direct call for violence ahead of the U.S. Capitol riot. The BBC acknowledged last month that the edit gave what it described as a mistaken impression. However, the corporation rejected demands for compensation and said there was no basis for a defamation claim.
