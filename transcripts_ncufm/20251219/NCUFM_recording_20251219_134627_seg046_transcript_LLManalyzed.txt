20251219 134627 By demonstrating that its sure result is misery and death, the unconditional pardon of sin never has been and never will be. Such pardon would show the abandonment of the principles of righteousness, which are the very foundation of the government of God. It would fill the whole, it would fill the unfallen universe with consternation. God has faithfully pointed out the results of sin. And if there, and if these warnings were not true, how could we be sure that his promises would be fulfilled? That so-called benevolence which would set aside justice is not benevolence but weakness. God is the life giver. From the beginning all his laws were ordained to life.
