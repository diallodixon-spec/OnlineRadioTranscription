The St. James Health Department says it is moving ahead with plans to rent garbage trucks to address ongoing waste collection issues across sections of the parish. Parish manager Lennox Wallace says 15 garbage trucks and two load vehicles are being brought in to carry out independent cleanup operations following concerns about uncollected waste before and after Hurricane Melissa. He says the initiative is being led solely by the health department due to delays in regular garbage collection. Wallace pointed to communities such as farm heights and areas of Montego Bay where garbage pile-up has contributed to an increase in rodents and public health concerns.
