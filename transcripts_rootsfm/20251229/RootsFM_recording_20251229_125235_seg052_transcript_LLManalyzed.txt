20251229 125235 Authorities in Turkey say they have detained 115 suspected members of the ISIS militant group during nationwide raids carried out ahead of the Christmas and New Year holiday period. According to the Istanbul Prosecutor's Office, arrest warrants were issued for 137 suspects after investigators uncovered information suggesting that ISIS operatives were planning attacks during the festive season. Prosecutors say the alleged plots were aimed at particularly non-Muslim individuals and public gatherings linked to Christmas and New Year celebrations. Police carried out coordinated operations at more than 120 coordinated locations across the country, detaining suspects and seizing firearms, ammunition, and what authorities described as organizational documents linked to the group.
