The first leg of the Christmas Village component of the Care Initiative was launched on December 6 in St. Elizabeth, where scores of children were treated to fun-filled games and received additional support services. The children were engaged in a simulated community experience, allowing them to visit the bank to withdraw JCF books before shopping for items of their choice.
