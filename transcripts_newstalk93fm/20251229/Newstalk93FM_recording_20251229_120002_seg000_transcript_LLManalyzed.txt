20251229 120002 This is the GOJ Minute for Monday, December 29. Bank of Jamaica Governor Richard Biles is encouraging the timely and targeted spending of hurricane relief funds, warning that delays could create inflationary pressures for the country. The Jamaica Agricultural Commodities Regulatory Authority has commenced the handover of 5,000 bags of fertilizer and essential agricultural inputs to coffee farmers to support recovery efforts post Hurricane Melissa. Rehabilitation works are underway on the Moye Hall Roadway in St. James, under the Shared Prosperity Through Accelerated Improvement to our Road Network Spark program. And Prime Minister Dr. Andrew Holdness says special provisions are being made to assist churches which suffered severe structural damage in the parishes hardest hit by Hurricane Melissa.
