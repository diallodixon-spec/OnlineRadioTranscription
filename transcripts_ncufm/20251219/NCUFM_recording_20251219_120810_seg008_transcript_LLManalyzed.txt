And the Ministry of Education, Skills, Youth and Information is inviting schools across the island to enter the upcoming Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics STEAM in Action Challenge. The competition being held under the theme Creating Solutions Driving Change is to be staged on January 29, 2026 as part of National STEAM Education Week, being observed from January 25 to 31, 2026. Participating schools will be required to showcase projects across the categories of renewable energy solutions, climate solutions, robotics and automation, health innovations, agriculture and food security, and environmental advocacy through the arts. Each school may submit up to three entries. Outstanding projects will be recognized at the national level with special acknowledgement given to initiatives that demonstrate creativity, innovation, collaboration, and community impact.
