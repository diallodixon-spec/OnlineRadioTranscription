20251212 135731 Jimmy Cliff's journey from Somerton to the world stage is written in the honors he received and the doors he opened. He was awarded Jamaica's Order of Merit in 2003, the highest honor our country can give in the arts. In 2010, he was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, where he is only one of two Jamaicans, the other being Bob Marley. The Hall hails him as reggae's first champion who carried the music across the globe. He was nominated for the Grammy Award seven times, and won twice for reggae album of the year. First for Cliffhanger in 1985 and again rebirth in 2012. Jimmy Cliff has taken his final bow but his voice will continue to echo in dance halls and churches and quiet moments of reflection.
