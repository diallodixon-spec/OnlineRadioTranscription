20251217 170403 Mr. Vaas says the target is to achieve about 90% power restoration by mid-January. In all, 300 linesmen will join JPS' local crew and will be assigned primarily to Westmoreland, St. Elizabeth, St. James, Hanover and Trelawny. The minister informed that engineering and planning personnel will be integrated immediately to support the redesign and rebuild of heavily damaged sections of the network. As of Tuesday December 16, 84% of JPS customers have been restored following the passage of Hurricane Melissa. Westmoreland has approximately 88% of customers still without service, St. Elizabeth 50%, St. James 45%, Trelawney 39% and Hanover 36%.
