20251219 195440 The final local swimming meet of 2025, the Christmas Open, splashes interaction this Saturday at the National Stadium Pool, bringing together a strong mix of international stars and overseas-based Caribbean swimmers. Defending champions Tornado Swim Club will be out to retain their title against stiff competition. Headlining the meet is Trinidad and Tobago's Zarek Wilson, the 2023 Commonwealth Youth Games gold medalist and a member of the Caribbean's fastest 4x100 meters freestyle relay team currently competing for the University of Alabama. He's quite a young swimmer.
