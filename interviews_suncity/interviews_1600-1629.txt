**Exclusive Interview with Dizzle J.A. on Sun City Radio**

In a captivating interview on Sun City Radio, host Chrisonic sat down with multi-talented musician Dizzle J.A. to discuss his latest projects, inspirations, and experiences. Born and raised in St. Thomas, Jamaica, Dizzle J.A. has been making waves in the music industry, both locally and internationally, with his unique sound and style.

The interview began with Dizzle J.A. sharing his early experiences with music, dancing to Michael Jackson as a child, and how those moments shaped his love for performing. He also spoke about the importance of his upbringing in St. Mary and St. Thomas, where he learned valuable lessons about resilience and perseverance.

Dizzle J.A. has been gaining recognition globally, with his music charting in multiple countries, including Finland, Morocco, and Africa. When asked about his international success, he humbly attributed it to the power of music, stating, "Music can travel, man. Yeah, music can travel, it's got wings." I said, look, the father just bring me through that." The song has been well-received by fans, with many praising its uplifting and motivational message.

A healthy lifestyle, we are promoting them things." With his inspiring story, talented music, and infectious personality, Dizzle J.A. is sure to continue making waves in the music industry and beyond.
