Here are four easy tips to take care of your health after the hurricane Mind your stress levels. Stress can make your pressure or sugar rise. So do some deep breathing. Pray. Take a walk. In other words, try to rest your mind. Stay connected by checking on your family and neighbors who are stronger together. Brought to you by the Ministry of Health.
