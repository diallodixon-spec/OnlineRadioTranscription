20251215 134729 A long time after that, the Lord had given rest unto Israel from all the enemies around, that Joshua waxed old and stricken in age. And Joshua called for all Israel, and for all the elders, and their heads, and for their judges, and for the officers, and said unto them, I am old and stricken in age, and ye have seen all that the Lord God hath done unto all these nations because of you. For the Lord your God is he who hath fought for you. Behold, I have divided unto you by lot these nations that remain to be an inheritance for you and for your tribes, from Jordan, with all the nations that I have cut off, even unto the great sea westward. And the LORD your God, he shall expel them from before you.
