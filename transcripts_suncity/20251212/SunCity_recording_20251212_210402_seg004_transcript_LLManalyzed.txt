His famous line that he would rather die free than live as someone's puppet became a watchword for personal and political dignity. He often said he was not a supporter of any political party. It is said that a prophet has no honor in his own country. Not so with Jimmy Cliff. Jimmy Cliff's journey from Somerton to the world stage is written in the honors he received and the doors he opened. He was awarded Jamaica's Order of Merit in 2003, the highest honour our country can give in the arts. In 2010 he was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame where he is only one of two Jamaicans, the other being Bob Marley.
