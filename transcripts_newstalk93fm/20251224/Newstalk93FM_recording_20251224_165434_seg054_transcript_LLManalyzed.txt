In the aftermath of Hurricane Melissa, recovery has meant more than restoring power lines and clearing roads. For thousands of micro, small and medium-sized enterprises across the island, especially in western Jamaica, it has been about reopening doors, keeping workers employed and rebuilding confidence. In doing so, they have been receiving support from the Ministry of Industry, Investment and Commerce, which has been coordinating across agencies to deliver practical support where it matters most.
