JCSD Trustee residents were told that they must prepare to relocate by the end of December, as the institution begins its cleanup ahead of the January school term. Yesterday, the anxious residents, who insisted that they were not leaving the shelter until authorities offered them a viable place to relocate, told Radio Jamaica News they had nowhere to go. In a statement today, Minister Mackenzie said there is no truth to reports that the government is moving to evict the Hurricane Melissa evacuees from the shelter. Mr. Mackenzie said his ministry is working closely with the Minister of Education and Works are already advanced to relocate the residents.
