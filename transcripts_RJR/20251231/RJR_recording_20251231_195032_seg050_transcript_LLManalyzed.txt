Even as it continues working to repair its damaged water systems in western Jamaica in the aftermath of Hurricane Melissa, the National Water Commission, NWC, continues to implement short-term measures to ensure persons in need can access the precious commodity. Among them is the trucking of water to communities all over Western Jamaica. Water has been trucked to communities in Hanover, including Horton Grove, Shecklewood Housing Scheme, Copps and Naukalva. In Westmoreland, areas served included Bethel Town, Darlingston Retreat, Red Road and Petersville.
