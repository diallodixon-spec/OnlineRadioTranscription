20251222 110103 Over 40 farmers in the Yalas Agri-Park in St. Thomas have received agricultural inputs under the Ministry of Agriculture's Hurricane Melissa recovery program. Fertilizer and seeds were distributed through the 1,000-acre boost program aimed at helping farmers recover following the passage of the hurricane. Senior Director for Agroparks and Production Zones at the Agroinvestment Corporation, Owen Scarlett, told Radio Jamaica News that Yalas is among more than 10 agroparks and 13 production zones benefiting island-wide. He added that close to 800 farmers are receiving seeds and fertilizer as part of the recovery.
