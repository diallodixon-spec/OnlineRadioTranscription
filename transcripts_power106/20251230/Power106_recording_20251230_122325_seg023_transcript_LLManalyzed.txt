Minister Sivrat also emphasized that while significant ground has been covered, recovery remains ongoing and sustained collaboration between government, the private sector and communities will be essential in the new year. The tourism product development company TP Deco has launched a mental health intervention program to provide psychosocial support to tourism workers affected by Hurricane Melissa. Executive tourism training manager at TP Deco Ruth Harris explains that psychologists will lead 10 on-site group sessions to help employees manage trauma, rebuild resilience, and return to full capacity. Ms. Harris adds that the program is targeting roughly 300 participants across 10 sessions and was organized in collaboration with the Jamaica Hotel and Tourist Association, JHTA.
