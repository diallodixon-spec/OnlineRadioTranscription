The government has begun road patching works in Kingston under the GO Road Rehabilitation Programme. Via a recent press release, the Ministry of Economic Growth and Infrastructure Development outlined that work has begun in the areas of Waterloo Road, East King's House Road, Mandela Highway, and several other priority corridors that require immediate repair to improve road safety and driving conditions. Minister with Responsibility for Works, Robert Morgan, further outlined that the GO program will expand beyond Kingston and move into other parishes in the coming days. Works will continue island-wide as part of a structured rollout aimed at restoring key routes and supporting daily commuting, commerce and public transportation.
