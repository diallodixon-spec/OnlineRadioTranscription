The police in St. James are reporting a 58% reduction in murders so far this year, when compared with the similar period last year. Divisional commander for St. James, Senior Superintendent Aaron Samuels, says 52 murders were recorded in the parish up to December 23, compared with 123 this time last year. SSP Samuels says the reduction is a signal of the work of the security forces in the parish. He adds that a significant number of murders committed in St. James so far this year have been solved.
